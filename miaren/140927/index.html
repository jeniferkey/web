Beginner Novice Horse Trial on Miaren

I felt it was a win for us that I even put Miaren on the trailer and headed to the event.
Our dressage was average, but I was so proud that we held it together through the warm up and
there were parts of our test that I was so happy with. We've done better, but not with that much excitement going on.
Our XC warm up was crazy and I had to keep my mouth shut so I wouldn't just scratch right there. Once on the course
we had quite a bit of fun and everything went well until the ditch with gravel in it. I did nurse him over after
a few stops, so we were out of the running, but we weren't eliminated and had one more day to play.
For stadium a friend's trainer stepped in for Meg, who was on her way on a cruise, and we tooled around the
intimidating course nicely. One rail, off a corner and the wrong lead, but we'd finished, which was the goal when we started.
