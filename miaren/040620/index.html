Miaren Playing in the Pasture
 
When I started to tell the horses to run, Miaren came up to me prancing and asking to play. I had to send him off and tell him that that wasn't what I meant by playing.
 
He's close to Thistle in height, but nowhere near the substance. We're separating him for feeding and he's getting as much as Emma, along with the vitamins. Still he doesn't have the weight of the others...
We'd be more worried about his ribs showing if he didn't have all the excess energy to run and play.
 
His trot is light and floaty, but on the forehand.
Catching up with Thistle.
 
He likes to flatten out in the field.
He really works all his muscles when he plays.
