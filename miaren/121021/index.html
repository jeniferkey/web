Novice Event at Curragh

Our dressage went great. We had energy and were relaxed. He could have been softer
to the bridle, but he knew jumping was coming up. Our score was a 31.
Stadium was a lot of fun. We were together on all our spots, I gave over fences and he
appreciated it and didn't do any head shaking. We may have looked a bit fast, but I let him pick his
pace and it never felt out of control. We were first after dressage and stadium. 
Cross country was a big question. We hadn't schooled the day before and we've had a lot of trouble
with ditches and banks in the past. I think that's all in the past. He cantered over the ditch and banks
and didn't seem to even consider them. We ended up with two refusals at separate jumps. One was in shadows
and he wanted to see it better, the other he wanted no part of, but reconsidered and took it for me from a trot. 
We didn't make time with the refusals, but we're getting closer. We cantered most of the course and did a bit of
galloping. I don't think the move up in height really concerned him, but he's not yet comfortable jumping a jump he's
not been shown. We got past the ditches, I think we can work through this. With all that we took home a 3rd place ribbon.
