Miaren's First Show
Miaren was just great. Loaded nicely for me alone, travelled well, and was wonderful for a
6 month old at his first show. He eyed everything new (not sure about walking on pavement),
but just started out talking a little to the other horses, and after an hour was neighing on occassion.
He relaxed and grazed in hand a little, accepted most of the petting and never made me worry
about control or being embarrassed.
The judge said that he had very nice gaits. A good dressage trot, and nice long walk. She said
that she feels he'll make a very nice dressage mount. He got his first blue ribbon,
but more than that his first outing was a good experience that makes me feel confident
about how he will behave in the future.
Practicing in-hand at home.
