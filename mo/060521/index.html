Mo - Training at the kennels
Mo is spending a month out at the kennels.
Chrissie is getting rides in on him and I'll hop on
him for roading and get some trail rides in.
We've got a hunt trail ride in June that I'm aiming for.
Mo doesn't seem to hold muscle easily. We're going to increase his oil and see if that helps. At least his endurance out on the trail seems just fine.
 

He's very obedient on the lunge line.
He's probably around 15.2, but much lighter bodied than my other draft crosses.
 
Chrissie's first time up.

He settled pretty quickly and stopped chewing.
