Niles - Fox Hunting
Niles' first time out fox hunting went very well.
He seems to love the excitement and the chance
to be out with his other horsey friends.
We hit some trails that were marked for the more
advanced horses, but Niles was brave enough to
cross the deep creek (with a jump and a splash)
and climbed his way up the rocky trails without hesitating.
He stayed with his group easily, happily trotting and
cantering along when necessary.
Next week he'll be braided up for opening meet. Hopefully
we'll have more pictures.
