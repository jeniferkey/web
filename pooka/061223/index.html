Pooka's Arrival
I'm very thankful that T'Lee let
me buy Pooka back after all these years.
I was looking for something smaller for
my niece to ride, and I think Pooka will be perfect.
There's a grin somewhere in that hood.
 
Amanda likes that she can reach Pooka's back for grooming and that he's just her height.
He's built like a small horse. Everything is smaller. His mother was that way.
 
Bravely going to meet the guys.
Nero's pretty enthralled with him. Pooka was just trying to look his biggest.
 
Checking out the fence line. 
For his size he can cover ground at the trot.
 
 He can also get those knees and hocks up when he wants to prance.
His pasturns are like springs. Lazer, his mother, could do the same thing. First they coil...
 
 Then they spring him forward.
A little playful cantering.
 
Showing off to the other horses.
