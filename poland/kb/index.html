Family in Poland
 
 
Yes!! I found someone. I wrote to one of the addresses on a letter that my grandmother had received in 1982. Aleksander Nozewski was the son of Stanislawa Maciejewska, my great-grandmother's sister.
From my letter I received a call from Zdzislawa, Alexander's daughter. She still lives in Kazimierez Biskupi with one of her daughters (who is about my age) and her family.
Her other daughter, Wanda, lives nearby with her family.
I went and visited them the first week of May. There were some communication problems, with the Polish/English barrier, but we still had a wonderful time.
