What We Think
Our course is very nice, but sometimes we play the stupid games. In our class is twelve kids and sweet teacher Jennifer.
We think that she likes us very much. We want some people in the United States her age, because Jennifer is in this class for her last semester. We don't want her to go on, but she must. We very love her, and she loves us too. But we will always write a letter to her.
In class, everyone likes everyone very much. We are a "crazy little class". That is our name. We are proud of us, because we are smart kids in big Poland.
We be in class because we want learn good English, and we want be in USA. Listen, talk and have some friends.
Anywhere, we be best friends and we want to be only in that class, and nothing else.
A Note from Jennifer
As you can tell, this is a sweet class to work with. Sometimes they talk to each other too much in Polish, but they are always eager to tell me about their lives and to explain Polish ideas.
I guess like in the States right now, they are all excited about Titanic, and all the girls love to listen to Celin Dion.
We laugh together at our stupid games of password and pictionary.
Starting next week, we hope to write email to pen pals in the United States.
