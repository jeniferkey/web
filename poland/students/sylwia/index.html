My hometown
Tomaszow is my hometown. It is 130 years old. 70,000 people live there. There are many places worth visiting. For instance, there is a big museum in which everyone can find out whole history about Tomaszow.There are also some parks and many historical buildings and monuments.In the southern part of the town there are hot springs. On one side, the town is surrounded by Pilica River and on the other side by a fresh-water bay.
