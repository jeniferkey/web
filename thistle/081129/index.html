2008-2009 Hunting Season



 
Always curious about the photographer.

Used to hounds everywhere.
 
He takes up some leg.
 
Taking the hounds away from the trailers.
 
Off on his own working with the hounds.
 
Working a scent.
 
 
Clipped in the winter.
 
Moving on at a trot.
 
Following the lead hounds.
 
On a run.
 
Gathering hounds back up at the water.
 
Waiting to unload.

Popping out of the woods.
 
Posing with his hounds.
 
Having a snack on the way in.
 
Back to the trailers.
