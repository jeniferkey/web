Thistle arriving at the Parents'
He and Skye seem to be very happy with each other's company
 
Yearlings are always a bit awkward, but there's a lot here that I like...
 
He's got some great legs. I like them solid with big joints.
He seems small until someone gets close to him. I'd guess he's around 14 hh now.
It took him a couple of days to get used to the place. Now he comes walking over any time he sees someone.
 
He's just a little on the thin side, but I think that's best at his age.
Yes, it is a theme that his head is down eating grass... 
He and Skye do some running and playing, but he spends most of his time grazing.
