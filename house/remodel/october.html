--October 2002--


We'd been debating and debating what to do with the counter. I started laying tile so I wouldn't have to think about it. Oct 2002

Father nudged us a bit with the faucet. Then he offered to come out and cut out the sink area so we could attempt a small repour. Oct 2002
 


This was Bart's original idea, and since I was getting ready to just use the broken counter, it seemed like the best plan. Father cut with a diamond blade. It took some time, but the cuts are nice and straight.
  


Here I have the sink form back in place and have laid out the rebar. The wood at the back will go in front for the lip form. The one thing I haven't found is the plastic pipe that went where the faucet goes...
