The Lone Star Hitch finds its home in the North Texas town of Weatherford. Its present mission is to promote the nationally known Kennedy's Sausage Company through the use of the largest horses on earth. Composed of six 2,000 lb. Percherons, the hitch travels thousands of miles each year entertaining young and old alike. Demand for appearances keeps them away from home about 150 days a year.


Weatherford Christmas Parade 2000

If you are fortunate enough to get to see Ken, his wife Shirley, their son Casey and these six beautiful Percheron horses performing during a rodeo, parading down Main Street or displayed at a local fair, you will be amazed that such creatures exist. Days after your experience you will still be wondering just how big and gentle these guys are. It will definitely support the old saying that, "Everything is bigger and better in Texas!"
