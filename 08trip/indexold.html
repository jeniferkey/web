2008 Trip

I hit a number of places this August.
The plan was to first head to Birmingham, UK and
tour around Shropshire and then head over to West/Northwest Wales.
Back on a plane to Denmark for a few days, and then back over
to hit the mountains further South in Wales, run over to Stratford Upon Avon
and ride the Severn Valley Railway, again in Shropshire.
I can't believe we packed everything in. Denmark was a nice relaxing break,
but I would have been hard pressed to do any more than all that we did. All of
my hosts were just amazing.
