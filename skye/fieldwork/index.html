Skye in the pasture
These are from video we took of Skye when we worked her loose in the pasture.
She's not as lazy as some of my other guys. I think she enjoyed the
attention we were giving her.
Trotting out a bit. 
 

Here she'd just done a flying change to the right. She's very comfortable with doing them on the straight-away when she's preparing to do a turn.
We've been working on the lunge in side reins. 
She handles them just fine, but is a bit more cautious about forward as she finds her balance while starting to give to the bit.
I try to keep the cantering on the lunge to a minimum. I'm also sure to give her quite a large circle to make it easier on her.
