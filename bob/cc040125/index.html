Cross Country at Meadow Creek

My first time back out cross country since last fall, and I had some
issues to face. I was too nervous to really want to jump.
We did make it around the gag stadium course with a clear round,
and I made it over two or three cross country jumps before my nerves
got the better of me. Emily was nice enough to get on and put him
over a few more jumps to show him why we were there.
I was pretty disappointed afterwards, but I think things will look up.
We did trot through the water.
