Kasza - Fox Hunting
Kasza, Adiron and Thuy all got to
go fox hunting this weekend. Kasza
didn't stay out long because we helped
another hunt rider, but she showed me that
she had the confidence to lead the hilltoppers
and not worry about catching up with the group
in front of us. It was a cold and windy day and she
was steady and forward. I couldn't ask for more.
She's got tons of experience standing at the trailer now. Adiron was a little nervous at the trailer, but she'd been out enough to know what was going to happen.
 

Kasza wasn't concerned about all the other horses. Adiron's little ears kept swiveling as he took everything in.
Kasza is great about listening to leg and is quite the bendy horse.
 
No fidgeting this morning while we waited for everyone to be saddled and ready.
Out hunting I don't worry about her softening and rounding up too much..
