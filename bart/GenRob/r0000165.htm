106. Sarah (Sallie)7 GRANBERRY (Loammi8) was born 12 DEC 1803. Sarah died 13 AUG 1853 at 49 years of age.

She married Asaph Powell ROBERTSON. Asaph was born in Rocky Comfort, Georgia 30 JAN 1803. Asaph(95) was the son of Norvell ROBERTSON and Sarah POWELL. Asaph died 19 SEP 1889 at 86 years of age. (See Asaph Powell ROBERTSON for the children resulting from this marriage.)
