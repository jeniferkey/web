93. Amanda Mortimer6 CHAMBERS (John7) was born 16 APR 1844. Amanda died 5 FEB 1906 at 61 years of age. Her body was interred in Kitchens Creek Cemetary, Pollock, Louisiana.

She married George Granberry ROBERTSON 5 MAR 1862. George was born in Covington County, Mississippi 16 JUL 1837. George(85) was the son of Asaph Powell ROBERTSON and Sarah (Sallie) GRANBERRY. George died 15 FEB 1902 in Louisiana, at 64 years of age. His body was interred in Kitchen Creek Cemetary, Pollock, Louisiana.
