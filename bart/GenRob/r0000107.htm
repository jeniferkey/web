158. Hough11 NORVILLE (George12, John13, Thomas14, John15)(124) birth date unknown.

He married Sara. Came to James City County before 1674. He succeeded his father as Vestryman, as his name appears frequently in parish records. His name is also found on the tablet erected in memory of vestrymen who erected the building 1710-1715. He was a member of the House of Burgesses. He was known as Captain Hough.

Hough NORVILLE and Sara had the following children:

159 i. Hough10 NORVILLE(125). Hough died 1747. Vestryman.

160 ii. William NORVILLE. He married Martha BUTLER.

161 iii. Sarah NORVILLE. She married George BASKERVILLE.

+ 146 iv. George NORVILLE was born 1700.
