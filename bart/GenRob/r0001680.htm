110. Marcia7 WELCH was born in Bryan County, Georgia 20 DEC 1814. Marcia died 13 FEB 1890 at 75 years of age.

She married John CHAMBERS. John was born 20 MAY 1794. (Additional notes for John CHAMBERS(99)) John died 27 NOV 1867 at 73 years of age. (See John CHAMBERS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
