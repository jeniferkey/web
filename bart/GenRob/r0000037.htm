Eleventh Generation

152. Jeoffrey11 ROBERTSON (Rev William12, John13, Thomas14, William15, William16, David17, Gilbert18, John19, Alexander20, Robert "Riach"21 DUNCANSON of STRUAN, Duncan22 of RANNOCH, Robert23 , Duncan "The Stout"24 of ATHOLL)(122) was born in Scotland 1654. Jeoffrey died 1734 in Henrico County, Virginia, America, at 80 years of age.

He married Elizabeth BOWMAN. Elizabeth is the daughter of John BOWMAN and Elizabeth BOWMAN. SOURCE NOTES: This information was sent to Peggy R. SANDIFER from Mae Rose ROBERTSON who obtained it through a library in Washington, DC from the Nottoway County Library copied from volumes of dittoed correspondence supplied by W. B. Sours, 1605 Aberdeen and Mercury Blvd., Hampton, VA 23366. It is believed that this Jeoffrey, with his elder brother, the Rev. George ROBERTSON, and others, patented a large tract of land in Henrico County, Virginia in 1703, to which Jeffery (Jeoffery) ROBERTSON was given a headright.

Jeoffrey ROBERTSON and Elizabeth BOWMAN had the following children:
