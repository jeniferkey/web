190. John15 NORVILLE(136) birth date unknown.

He married an unknown person. The name NORVILLE was originally French -- Neufuille or Neuville. John and Thomas NORVILLE may have been brothers. They came to Isle of Wight County, Virginia in 1622. (Settled between April 27, 1619 and November 2, 1621.)

John NORVILLE had the following child:

+ 183 i. Thomas14 NORVILLE.
