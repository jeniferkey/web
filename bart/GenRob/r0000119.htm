133. Sarah9 NORVILLE (George10, Hough11, George12, John13, Thomas14, John15) was born 9 SEP 1730. Sarah died 17 SEP 1812 at 82 years of age.

She married Jeffery ROBERTSON III 1757. Jeffery was born in Chesterfield County, Virginia 15 JAN 1737. Jeffery(113) was the son of Jeffery ROBERTSON II and Judith TANNER. Jeffery died 2 APR 1827 in Buckingham, Virginia, at 90 years of age. (See Jeffery ROBERTSON III for the children resulting from this marriage.)
