122. Sarah8 POWELL (Benjamin9) was born in South Carolina 7 FEB 1770. Sarah died 24 OCT 1842 at 72 years of age.

She married Norvell ROBERTSON in Georgia, 7 JUL 1791. Norvell was born 22 MAY 1765. Norvell(111) was the son of Jeffery ROBERTSON III and Sarah NORVILLE. (See Norvell ROBERTSON for the children resulting from this marriage.)
