Twentieth Generation

196. Alexander20 ROBERTSON {5th Baron of Struan} (Robert "Riach"21 DUNCANSON of STRUAN, Duncan22 of RANNOCH, Robert23 , Duncan "The Stout"24 of ATHOLL)(140) birth date unknown. Alexander died 1505.

He married twice. He married Isabelle STEWART. Isabelle was the daughter of Sir_John STEWART of Balveny 1st Earl of Atholl and Eleanor SINCLAIR. He married Elizabeth LYON of Glamis. Chief of Clan Donnachaidh. Adopted the surname "ROBERTSON."

Alexander ROBERTSON 5th Baron of Struan and Isabelle STEWART had the following children:

+ 195 i. John19 ROBERTSON.

197 ii. Duncan ROBERTSON.

198 iii. Robert ROBERTSON. He married an unknown person.

199 iv. Andrew ROBERTSON(141). SOURCE NOTES: From chart of Clan Donnachaidh chiefs provided by Clan Museum & Centre. From whom descend the Robertsons of Killiechangie, Edradynate, Ladykirk, and Cultalonie.

200 v. James ROBERTSON(142). SOURCE NOTES: From chart of Clan Donnachaidh chiefs provided by Clan Museum & Centre. From whom descend the Robertsons of Auchleeks, Calvine, Blairfetty, Trinafour, and Kindrochit.

201 vi. Alexander ROBERTSON(143). SOURCE NOTES: From chart of Clan Donnachaidh chiefs provided by Clan Museum & Centre. From whom descend the Robertsons of Faskally, Tenandry, and Balnacraig.

Alexander ROBERTSON 5th Baron of Struan and Elizabeth LYON of Glamis had the following child:

202 vii. Robert ROBERTSON. He married Elizabeth STEWART. Elizabeth is the daughter of Sir_John STEWART of Balveny 1st Earl of Atholl and Eleanor SINCLAIR.
