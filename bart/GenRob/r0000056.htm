144. Judith10 TANNER was born about 1710.

She married twice. She married unknown MILLS. She married Jeffery ROBERTSON II 1734 (1735). Jeffery was born 1709. Jeffery(116) was the son of Jeoffrey ROBERTSON and Elizabeth BOWMAN. Jeffery died 16 DEC 1784 at 75 years of age.

Judith TANNER and unknown MILLS had the following child:

145 i. Henry9 MILLS.
