Twenty-Fourth Generation

217. Duncan "The Stout"24 of ATHOLL {1st Baron of Struan}(153) birth date unknown. Duncan died 1314 in Bannockburn.

He married an unknown person. SOURCE NOTES: From chart of Clan Donnachaidh chiefs provided by Clan Museum & Centre. Chief of Clan Donnachaidh. It was claimed that Duncan picked up a rock along the road to Bannockburn, predicting victory for the Scottish forces. This rock is now in care of the Clan museum.
