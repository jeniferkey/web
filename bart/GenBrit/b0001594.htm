146. Marie16 deLORRAINE d'AUMALE (Claude17 )(78) birth date unknown. Marie died 10 JUN 1560 in Edinburgh Castle, Scotland. Her body was interred in Rheims, France.

She married JAMES V STEWART, King of Scots JUN 1538. JAMES was born 10 APR 1512. JAMES was the son of JAMES IV STEWART King of Scots and Margaret TUDOR Princess of England. JAMES died 14 DEC 1542 in Falkland, Scotland, at 30 years of age. His body was interred in Holyroodhouse, Edinburgh, Scotland. (See JAMES V STEWART, King of Scots for the children resulting from this marriage.) of Lorraine-Guise
