705. Lady_Maud30 deCAEN of GLOUCESTER (Robert "The Counsel"30 deCAEN, 1st Earl of GLOUCESTER) birth date unknown. Lady_Maud died 29 JUL 1189.

She married Ranulf "de Briquessard" deGUERNAN, 2nd Earl of CHES about 1141. Ranulf was born in Castle Guernon, Normandy CA 1109(10). Ranulf was the son of Ranulf "de Briquessart" deMESCHIN 1st Earl of CHES and Lucia (Lucy) TAILLEBOIS. Ranulf died (6)16 DEC 1189. His body was interred in St.Werburg, s, Chester, England. (See Ranulf "de Briquessard" deGUERNAN, 2nd Earl of CHES for the children resulting from this marriage.)
