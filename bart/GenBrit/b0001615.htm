44. EDWARD VII Albert Guelph6 SAXE-COBURG (Albert Augustus7 of SAXE-COBURG-GOTHA, Ernest I8 ) was born in Buckingham Pal., London, England 9 NOV 1841. Individual flags: KingGrBr. EDWARD died 6 MAY 1910 in Buckingham Palace, at 68 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor.

He married Alexandra of Denmark in St.George's Chap, Windsor, Berks., England, 10 MAR 1863. Alexandra was born in Yellow Palace, Copenhagen, Denmark 1 DEC 1844. Alexandra was the daughter of Christian IX of Denmark and Louise. Alexandra died 20 NOV 1925 in Sandringham House, Norfolk, England, at 80 years of age. Her body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor.
