Individual flags: KingScot; KingEng. JAMES died 27 MAR 1625 in Theobalds, Herts., England, at 58 years of age. His body was interred in Westminster Abbey.

He married Anne of Denmark in Uppsala, Sweden, 21 NOV 1589. Anne was born in Skanderborg Castle, Jutland, Denmark 14 OCT 1574. Anne was the daughter of Frederick II and Sophia. Anne died 2 MAR 1619 in Hampton Court, Bucks., England, at 44 years of age. Her body was interred in Westminster Abbey. After ruling as king in Scotland following the execution of his mother, Mary, James was made King of England as well in 1603 when Elizabeth died.
