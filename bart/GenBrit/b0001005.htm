1107. St. Alfgifu (Elfgiva)35 (832) birth date unknown. St. died 944. Her body was interred in Shaftsbury, Dorset.

She married EDMUND I "The Magnificent". EDMUND was born 920. EDMUND(833) was the son of EDWARD I "The Elder" and Eadgifu (Egwina) MEAPHAM of KENT. EDMUND died 26 MAY 946 in Pucklechurch, Gloucestershire. His body was interred in Glastonbury, Abbeyal, London, Eng. (See EDMUND I "The Magnificent" for the children resulting from this marriage.) also rendered as Elgifu or Elfgiva (means "Faerie's Gift")
