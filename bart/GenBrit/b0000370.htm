619. "Red" Eva29 MacMORROUGH(421) birth date unknown. "Red" died about 1177.

She married Richard FitzGilbert "Strongbow" deCLARE, 2rd Earl of PEMBROKE 26 AUG 1171 (1170). Richard was born about 1130. Richard was the son of Gilbert Fitzgilbert deCLARE 1st Earl of PEMBROKE and Isabel (Elizabeth) deBEAUMONT. Richard died 20 APR 1176(77) in Ireland. His body was interred in Cathedral Church of Dublin. (See Richard FitzGilbert "Strongbow" deCLARE, 2rd Earl of PEMBROKE for the children resulting from this marriage.) dau. of King of Leinster
