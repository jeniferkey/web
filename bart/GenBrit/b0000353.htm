563. Maud (Matilda)28 deGUERNAN of CHESTER (Hugh29 deGUERNAN, 3rd Earl of CHESTER, Ranulf "de Briquessard"30 deGUERNAN, 2nd Earl of CHESTER)(375) was born 1171. Maud died 1233 at 62 years of age.

She married David MacHENRY, Earl of HUNTINGDON & CHESTER 26 AUG 1190. David was born 1144. David was the son of Prince Henry MacDAVID Earl of HUNTINGDON and Adeleine (Ada) deWARENNE. David died 17 JUN 1219 in Yardley, England, at 74 years of age. (See David MacHENRY, Earl of HUNTINGDON & CHESTER for the children resulting from this marriage.) of Kevilioc
