505. Adeleine (Ada)28 deWARENNE (William29 deWARENNE, 2nd Earl of SURREY, William30 deWARENNE, 1st Earl of SURREY, Rodulf31 deWARENNE)(334) birth date unknown. Adeleine died 1178.

She married Prince Henry MacDAVID, Earl of HUNTINGDON 1139. Prince was born 1114. Prince(335) was the son of DAVID I and Matilda (Maud) of HUNTINGDON. Prince died 12 JUN 1152 at 37 years of age. (See Prince Henry MacDAVID, Earl of HUNTINGDON for the children resulting from this marriage.) of Surrey
