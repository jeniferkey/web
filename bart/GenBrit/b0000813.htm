1153. Matilda (Mahaud)35 deBOURGOGNE (BURGUNDY) (Herman36 of SAXONY, Billung37 of STUBECKESHORN) birth date unknown. Matilda died 25 MAY 1008.

She married (Baudoin) Baldwin III Count of Flanders. (Baudoin)(878) was the son of Arnulf I (Arnold) "The Old" Count of Flanders & ARTOIS and Alix (Adelle) deVERMANDOIS. (Baudoin) died 1 JAN 962. (See (Baudoin) Baldwin III Count of Flanders for the children resulting from this marriage.)
