409. Lady_Margaret26 STEWART (Walter26 STEWART, 3rd High Steward of Scotland, Alan27 FITZWALTER, 2nd High Steward of Scotland, Walter28 FITZALAN, 1st High Steward of Scotland, Alan29 FITZFLAALD, Lord OSWESTRY, Flaald30 FITZGUIENOC, Guienoc31 FITZHAMMO, HAMO32 ) birth date unknown.

She married Nigel MacDUNCAN, 2nd Earl of CARRICK. Nigel(301) was the son of Duncan 1st Earl of CARRICK and Avelina. Nigel died 1256. (See Nigel MacDUNCAN, 2nd Earl of CARRICK for the children resulting from this marriage.)
