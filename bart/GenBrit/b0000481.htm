499. Prince Henry28 MacDAVID, Earl of HUNTINGDON (DAVID I29 , MALCOLM III "Canmore"30, DUNCAN I31 MacCRINAN, CRINAN "The Thane"32 )(329) was born 1114. Prince died 12 JUN 1152 at 37 years of age.

He married Adeleine (Ada) deWARENNE 1139. Adeleine(330) was the daughter of William deWARENNE 2nd Earl of SURREY and Isabel deVERMANDOIS. Adeleine died 1178. Earl of Northumberland
