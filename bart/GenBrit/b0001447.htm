205. Mary19 deGUELDRES birth date unknown. Mary died 3 JUL 1449.

She married JAMES II STEWART, King of Scots 3 JUL 1449. JAMES was born 16 OCT 1430. JAMES was the son of JAMES I STEWART King of Scots and Joan BEAUFORT of DORSET. JAMES died 3 AUG 1460 in Roxburghe Castle, at 29 years of age.(121) His body was interred in Holyrood Castle, Edinburgh, Scotland. (See JAMES II STEWART, King of Scots for the children resulting from this marriage.)
