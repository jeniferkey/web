67. Augusta Wilhelmina Louisa7 of HESSE-CASSEL (Frederick8 ) was born in Rumpenheim 25 JUL 1797. Augusta died 6 APR 1889 in St. James's Palace, at 91 years of age.

She married Adolphus Frederick HANOVER in Buckingham House, 1 JUN 1818. Adolphus was born in Buckingham House 24 FEB 1774. Adolphus was the son of GEORGE III HANOVER and Charlotte Sophia of MECKLENBERG-STRELITZ. Adolphus died 8 JUL 1850 in Cambridge House, Piccadilly, at 76 years of age. (See Adolphus Frederick HANOVER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
