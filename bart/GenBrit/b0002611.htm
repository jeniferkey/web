215. Margaret20 BEAUCHAMP birth date unknown.

She married John BEAUFORT, Duke of SOMERSET. John was born 1403. John was the son of John BEAUFORT Marquess of DORSET and Margaret HOLLAND. John died 1444 at 41 years of age. (See John BEAUFORT, Duke of SOMERSET for the children resulting from this marriage.)
