735. Adelaide31 deVEXIN (Raoul III "The Great"32 ) birth date unknown.

She married Herbert IV deVERMANDOIS. Herbert was born about 1032. Herbert was the son of Otho (Otto) deVERMANDOIS and Parvie. Herbert died about 1080. (See Herbert IV deVERMANDOIS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
