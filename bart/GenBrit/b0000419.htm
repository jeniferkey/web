710. Maud (Mabel)30 FITZHAMMO of GLOUCESTER (Robert31 FITZHAMMO, Earl of GLOUCESTER) birth date unknown.

She married Robert "The Counsel" deCAEN, 1st Earl of GLOUCESTER about 1120. Robert was born about 1090.(500) Robert(501) was the son of HENRY I "The Lion of Justice" and unknown. Robert died 31 OCT 1147 in Bristol, England, at 57 years of age. (See Robert "The Counsel" deCAEN, 1st Earl of GLOUCESTER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
