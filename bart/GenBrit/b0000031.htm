James24 STEWART, 5th High Steward of Scotland (Alexander25 STEWART, 4th High Steward of Scotland, Walter26 STEWART, 3rd High Steward of Scotland, Alan27 FITZWALTER, 2nd High Steward of Scotland, Walter28 FITZALAN, 1st High Steward of Scotland, Alan29 FITZFLAALD, Lord OSWESTRY, Flaald30 FITZGUIENOC, Guienoc31 FITZHAMMO, HAMO32 ) was born about 1243. James died 16 JUL 1309 at 66 years of age.

He married Egidia deBURGH. Egidia is the daughter of Walter deBURGH and Avelina (FitzJohn) FITZGEOFFERY. Succeeded as 5th High Steward 1283. He was one of the six magnates of Scotland chosen to act as regents of the Kingdom. Submitted to Edward I, 9th JUL 1297.
