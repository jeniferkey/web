998. Aelfgar III33 MALET of MERCIA (Leofric34 of MERCIA, Leofwine35, Dulf36 of DEVON)(729) was born about 1031. Aelfgar died CA 1062(59).

He married Elfgifu. Elfgifu is the daughter of ETHELRED II "The Unready" and Elflaed. Earl of E. Anglia, 1053; Earl of Mercia, 1057
