543. Roger28 leBIGOD, Earl of NORFOLK (Hugh29 leBIGOD, Roger30, Robert31, Robert32, Turstain33 deGOZ, 3rd Vicomte d'EXMES, Onfroy34 deGOZ, 2nd Vicomte d'EXMES, Ansfrid "The Danish"35 deGOZ, 1st Vicomte d'EXMES, Hrolf36 TURSTAIN, Hrollager37 of MAERC (BIGOD))(362) was born about 1150.
