He married Hawise (Hawice) deBEAUMONT about 1150. Hawise was the daughter of Robert deBEAUMONT and Petronilla leGRANTMESNIL. Hawise died 24 APR 1197. Count of Meullent; Lord of Glamorgan & Cardiff Castle
