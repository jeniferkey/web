She married HENRY I "The Lion of Justice" MISTRESS. HENRY was born in Selby, Yorkshire SEP 1068. HENRY(504) was the son of WILLIAM I "The Conqueror" 7th Duke of Normandy and Matilda (Maud) of Flanders. HENRY died 1 DEC 1135 in St. Denis-le-Fermont, near Gisors, at 67 years of age.(505) His body was interred in Reading Abbey.
