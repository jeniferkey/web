CHARLES "The Bald"37 (LOUIS I "The Pious" "The Fair"38, CHARLEMAGNE39, PEPIN "The Short"40, Charles41 MARTEL, Pepin II "The Younger"42 of HERISTAL, Ansgise (Ansegis)43 , Arnulf44, Bodegisel II45 of METZ, Bodegisel I46 , Munderic47 of VITRY-EN-PERTHOIS, CLODERIC "The Parricide"48 , SIGEBERT "The Lame"49, CHILDEBERT50 of COLOGNE, CLOVIS "The Riparian"51)(967) was born in Frankfort-on-Maine 13 JUN 828. CHARLES died 6 OCT 877 at 49 years of age.

He married Ermentrude of Orleans 14 DEC 842. Ermentrude was the daughter of Odo of Orleans and Engeltrude. Ermentrude died 6 OCT 869. King of France; Emperor 25 DEC 875-877

CHARLES "The Bald" and Ermentrude of Orleans had the following children:

+ 1197 i. Hersent36 of France.

+ 1255 ii. Judith of France was born 846.

+ 1256 iii. LOUIS II "The Stammerer" was born 844.
