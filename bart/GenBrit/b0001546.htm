Archibald died 22 JAN 1556/1557.

He married Margaret TUDOR, Princess of England in Kinnoull, 4 AUG 1514. Margaret was born in Palace of Westminster 28 NOV 1489. Margaret was the daughter of HENRY VII TUDOR Earl of RICHMOND and Elizabeth PLANTAGENET of YORK. Margaret died 18 OCT 1541 in Methven Castle, at 51 years of age.
