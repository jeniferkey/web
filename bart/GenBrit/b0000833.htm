Adela died 8 JAN 1079.

She married Baldwin V "De Lilles" of Flanders. Baldwin was born 1012. Baldwin(616) was the son of Baldwin IV "The Bearded" of Flanders and Ogiva (Cunegonde) of Luxembourg. Baldwin died 1 SEP 1067 in Lilles, France, at 55 years of age. His body was interred in Collegiate Church of St. Pierr.
