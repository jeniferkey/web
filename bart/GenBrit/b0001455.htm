238. Margaret21 HAY of YESTER (Sir_William22) birth date unknown.

She married William DOUGLAS, 2nd Earl of ANGUS 3 DEC 1414. William was the son of George DOUGLAS 1st Earl of ANGUS and Mary STEWART Princess of Scotland. William died about 1437. (See William DOUGLAS, 2nd Earl of ANGUS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
