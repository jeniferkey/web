1221. Adele (Aelis)36 birth date unknown.

She married ROBERT CAPET. ROBERT was born 866.(935) ROBERT(936) was the son of Rutpert IV "The Strong" of RHINE & WORMAGAU and Aelis of TOURS and ALSACE. ROBERT died 15 JUN 923.(937) (See ROBERT CAPET for the children resulting from this marriage.)
