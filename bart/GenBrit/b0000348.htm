608. Adela (Adeliza)29 deMESCHIN of CHESTER (Ranulf "de Briquessart"30 deMESCHIN, 1st Earl of CHES, Ranulf31 deMESCHIN, Vicomte de BAYEUX, Ranulf32 Comte de BAYEUX) birth date unknown. Adela died 1128.

She married Richard Fitzgilbert deCLARE, 1st Earl of HEREFORD. Richard was born before 1105. Richard was the son of Gilbert Fitzrichard deCLARE 2nd Earl of CLARE and Adeliza (Alice Adelaide) deCLERMONT. Richard died 15 APR 1136 at 32 years of age. (See Richard Fitzgilbert deCLARE, 1st Earl of HEREFORD for the children resulting from this marriage.)
