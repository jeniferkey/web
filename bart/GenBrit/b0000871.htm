946. HWYEL DDA "The Good"33 apCADELL (Cadell34 apRHODRI, RHODRI MAWR "The Great"35 of WALES, MERFYN FRYCH "The Freckled"36 , GWRIAD37 of MANAU)(688) birth date unknown. HWYEL died 950.

He married Elen of DYFED. Elen(689) is the daughter of Llywarch apHYFAIDD. Ruler of Seisyllwg; obtained kingdom of Dyfed through wife. The combined territories of Dyfed and Seisyllwg became known as Deheubarth.
