153. Margaret17 TUDOR, Princess of England (HENRY VII18 TUDOR, Earl of RICHMOND, Edmund19, Owain20 TUDOR, Maredudd21 apTUDUR, Tudur22 apGORONWY, Goronwy23 apTUDUR, Tudur24 apGORONWY, Goronwy25 apEDNYFED, Ednyfed Fychan26 ) was born in Palace of Westminster 28 NOV 1489. Margaret died 18 OCT 1541 in Methven Castle, at 51 years of age.

She married three times. She married JAMES IV STEWART, King of Scots in Holyrood House, 8 AUG 1503. JAMES was born 17 MAR 1472/1473. JAMES was the son of JAMES III STEWART King of Scots and Margaret of Denmark. JAMES died 9 SEP 1513 in Flodden, at 40 years of age.(82) His body was interred in Richmond, Surrey, England. She married Archibald DOUGLAS, 6th Earl of ANGUS in Kinnoull, 4 AUG 1514. Archibald was the son of George DOUGLAS Master of ANGUS and Elizabeth DRUMMOND of DRUMMOND. Archibald died 22 JAN 1556/1557. She married Henry STEWART, 1st Lord METHVEN MAR 1528. Henry died 1557.
