4. Diana Frances2 SPENCER, Princess of Wales (Edward John3 SPENCER, 8th Earl SPENCER, John4 SPENCER, 7th Earl SPENCER) was born in Park House, Sandringham, Norfolk 1 JUL 1961. Diana died 31 AUG 1997 in Paris, France, at 36 years of age. Killed in car accident, along with boyfriend Dodi Al Fayed (movie producer and heir to Harrod's stores) and chauffeur. The car in which the princess was riding was being driven at high speeds to evade a number of following vehicles containing press and photographers.

She married Charles Philip Arthur George WINDSOR 29 JUL 1981.(2) RESEARCH NOTES: Divorced 1996.

Charles was born in Buckingham Palace 14 NOV 1948. Charles is the son of Philip MOUNTBATTEN Duke of Edinburgh and ELIZABETH II WINDSOR Queen of England. (See Charles Philip Arthur George WINDSOR for the children resulting from this marriage.)
