1027. ELFRIDA34 of DEVON (Ordgar35)(756) was born 945. ELFRIDA died 1000.

She married twice. She married EDGAR "The Peaceful" 965(64). EDGAR was born about 943. EDGAR(757) was the son of EDMUND I "The Magnificent" and St. Alfgifu (Elfgiva). EDGAR died 8 JUL 975 at 32 years of age. His body was interred in Glastonbury Abbey. She married Ethelwold. 1st Saxon queen to be anointed & crowned
