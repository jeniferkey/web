523. Eleanor28 of AQUITAINE (William X29) was born in Bordeaux or Belin about 1122. Eleanor died 31 MAR 1204 in Fontevraud, at 81 years of age. Her body was interred in Fontevraud Abbey.

She married twice. She married LOUIS VII King of France in Bordeaux, 1137.(351) RESEARCH NOTES: (annulled)

She married HENRY II PLANTAGENET in Bordeaux, 18 MAY 1152.(352) RESEARCH NOTES: (annulled)

HENRY was born in Le Mans 25 MAR 1133. HENRY(353) was the son of Geoffrey V PLANTAGENET of ANJOU and Matilda. HENRY died 6 JUL 1189 in Chinon, at 56 years of age. His body was interred in Fontevraud Abbey.
