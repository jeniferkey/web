420. HENRY III26 PLANTAGENET (JOHN "Lackland"27, HENRY II28, Geoffrey V29 PLANTAGENET of ANJOU, FULK V "The Young"30 of ANJOU, Fulk IV "Rechin"31, Aubri-Geoffrey32 of the GATINAIS, Geoffrey III33)(287) was born in Winchester Castle 1 OCT 1207. HENRY died 16 NOV 1272 in Palace of Westminster, at 65 years of age. His body was interred in Westminster Abbey.

He married Eleanor of PROVENCE in Canterbury Cathedral, 4 JAN 1236. Eleanor was born in Aix-en-Provence about 1223. Eleanor was the daughter of Raymond IV BERENGER Count of PROVENCE and Beatrice of SAVOY. Eleanor died 24 JUN 1292 in Amesbury, Wiltshire, at 68 years of age. Her body was interred in Convent Church, Amesbury.(288) King of England 1216-1272
