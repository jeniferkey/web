962. Ogiva (Cunegonde)33 of Luxembourg (Frederick34) birth date unknown. Ogiva died 21 FEB 1030.

She married Baldwin IV "The Bearded" of Flanders 1012. Baldwin was born 980. Baldwin(701) was the son of (Arnold) Arnulf II "The Young" Count of Flanders and Rosele (Susanna).
