Ranulf "de Briquessard"30 deGUERNAN, 2nd Earl of CHESTER (Ranulf "de Briquessart"30 deMESCHIN, 1st Earl of CHESTER, Ranulf31 deMESCHIN, Vicomte de BAYEUX, Ranulf32 Comte de BAYEUX) was born in Castle Guernon, Normandy about 1109(10). Ranulf died (6)16 DEC 1189. His body was interred in St.Werburg, s, Chester, England.

He married Lady_Maud deCAEN of GLOUCESTER about 1141. Lady_Maud was the daughter of Robert "The Counsel" deCAEN 1st Earl of GLOUCESTER and Maud (Mabel) FITZHAMMO of GLOUCESTER. Lady_Maud died 29 JUL 1189.
