65. Adolphus Frederick7 HANOVER (GEORGE III8, Frederick Louis9 HANOVER, Prince of Wales, GEORGE II Augustus Guelph10 HANOVER, GEORGE I Louis Guelph11, Ernest Augustus12 Duke of BRUNSWICK-LUNEBURG) was born in Buckingham House 24 FEB 1774. Adolphus died 8 JUL 1850 in Cambridge House, Piccadilly, at 76 years of age.

He married Augusta Wilhelmina Louisa of HESSE-CASSEL in Buckingham House, 1 JUN 1818. Augusta was born in Rumpenheim 25 JUL 1797. Augusta was the daughter of Frederick and Caroline. Augusta died 6 APR 1889 in St. James's Palace, at 91 years of age.

Adolphus Frederick HANOVER and Augusta Wilhelmina Louisa of HESSE-CASSEL had the following children:

66 i. George6 HANOVER, Duke of CAMBRIDGE was born 1819. George died 1904 at 85 years of age.

+ 51 ii. Joan HANOVER, Princess Mary of CAMBRIDGE was born 1833.
