Raymond IV BERENGER, Count of PROVENCE and Beatrice of SAVOY had the following children:

464 i. Margaret26 of PROVENCE.

+ 465 ii. Beatrix of PROVENCE.

+ 429 iii. Eleanor of PROVENCE was born about 1223.

466 iv. Sanchia of PROVENCE was born in Aix-en-Provence about 1225. Sanchia died 9 NOV 1261 in Berkhamstead, at 36 years of age. She married RICHARD PLANTAGENET, Earl of CORNWALL in Westminster Abbey, 23 NOV 1243. RICHARD was born in Winchester Castle 5 JAN 1209. RICHARD(315) was the son of JOHN "Lackland" PLANTAGENET and Isabelle TAILLEFER of ANGOULEME. RICHARD died 2 APR 1272 in Berkhamstead, at 63 years of age.
