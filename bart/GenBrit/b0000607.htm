1132. Hrolf (Rollo) (Robert I) "The Ganger"35 (Rognwald36 Jarl of MAERC, Eyestein37 Jarl of MEARC, Ivar38 Earl of the Uplands, Halfdan "The Old"39 , SVEIDE "The Viking"40)(856) was born 846. Hrolf died 931(32) in Rouen. His body was interred in Cathedral at Rouen.

He married Lady Popa (Papie) deVALOIS.(857) Hrolf later repudiated what was considered his "pagan" marriage to Lady Popa(858), the daughter of Pepin deSENLIS Count Berengarius of BRITTANY and unknown. A Norse Viking called "The Walker", he became the 1st Duke of Normandy.
