284. Isabel22 of CASTILE birth date unknown. Isabel died 23 NOV 1393.

She married Edmund PLANTAGENET of LANGLEY, Duke of YORK in Hertford, about 1 MAR 1372. Edmund was born in King's Langley, Hertfordshire 5 JUN 1341. Edmund was the son of EDWARD III PLANTAGENET and Philippa of HAINAULT. Edmund died 1 AUG 1402 in King's Langley, at 61 years of age. (See Edmund PLANTAGENET of LANGLEY, Duke of YORK for the children resulting from this marriage.)
