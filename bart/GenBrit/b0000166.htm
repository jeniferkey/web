713. Petronilla30 leGRANTMESNIL (Hugh31, Robert32, Hugh33 leGRANTMESNIL, Count of GRANTMESNIL) birth date unknown.

She married Robert deBEAUMONT about 1155. Robert was born before 1135. Robert(503) was the son of Robert "Robin the Hunchback" deBEAUMONT 2nd Earl of LEICESTER and Amice deMONTFORT. Robert died 1190 in Durazzo, Greece, at 56 years of age. (See Robert deBEAUMONT for the children resulting from this marriage.)
