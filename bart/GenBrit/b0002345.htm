429. Eleanor26 of PROVENCE (Raymond IV27 BERENGER, Count of PROVENCE) was born in Aix-en-Provence about 1223. Eleanor died 24 JUN 1292 in Amesbury, Wiltshire, at 68 years of age. Her body was interred in Convent Church, Amesbury.(293) RESEARCH NOTES: Her heart is kept in the Church of Friars Minor, London.

She married HENRY III PLANTAGENET in Canterbury Cathedral, 4 JAN 1236. HENRY was born in Winchester Castle 1 OCT 1207. HENRY(294) was the son of JOHN "Lackland" PLANTAGENET and Isabelle TAILLEFER of ANGOULEME. HENRY died 16 NOV 1272 in Palace of Westminster, at 65 years of age. His body was interred in Westminster Abbey. (See HENRY III PLANTAGENET for the children resulting from this marriage.)
