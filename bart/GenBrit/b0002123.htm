192. EDWARD IV19 PLANTAGENET, Earl of MARCH (Richard20 PLANTAGENET, Duke of YORK, Richard21 PLANTAGENET, Earl of CAMBRIDGE, Edmund22 PLANTAGENET of LANGLEY, Duke of YORK, EDWARD III23 PLANTAGENET, EDWARD II24, EDWARD I "Longshanks"25, HENRY III26, JOHN "Lackland"27, HENRY II28, Geoffrey V29 PLANTAGENET of ANJOU, FULK V "The Young"30 of ANJOU, Fulk IV "Rechin"31, Aubri-Geoffrey32 of the GATINAIS, Geoffrey III33)(113) was born in Rouen, France 28 APR 1442. EDWARD died 9 APR 1483 in Palace of Westminster, at 40 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor Castle.

He married Elizabeth WOODVILLE in Grafton Regis, 1 MAY 1464. Elizabeth was born in Grafton Regis, Northants about 1437. Elizabeth(114) was the daughter of Sir_Richard WOODVILLE 1st Earl RIVERS and Jacquetta of Luxembourg. Elizabeth died 8 JUN 1492 in Bermondsey Abbey, at 54 years of age. Her body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor. King of England 1461-1483; crowned 4 MAR 1461; deposed 3 OCT 1470; restored 11 APR 1471.

EDWARD IV PLANTAGENET, Earl of MARCH and Elizabeth WOODVILLE had the following children:

+ 171 i. Elizabeth18 PLANTAGENET of YORK was born 11 FEB 1466.

193 ii. Mary PLANTAGENET was born in Windsor Castle AUG 1466. Mary died 23 MAY 1482 in Greenwich, at 15 years of age.

194 iii. Cicely PLANTAGENET was born 20 MAR 1469. Cicely died 24 AUG 1507 in Quarr Abbey, Isle of Wight, at 38 years of age. She married twice. She married John WELLES, 1st Viscount WELLES before DEC 1487. John died 9 FEB 1499 in London. She married Thomas KYME before JAN 1504.

195 iv. EDWARD V PLANTAGENET(115) was born in Sanctuary at Westminster 4 NOV 1470. EDWARD died about 1483 in place of death uncertain. Deposed 25 JUN 1483 (never crowned)

196 v. Margaret PLANTAGENET was born 10 APR 1472. Margaret died 11 DEC 1472 at less than one year of age.

197 vi. Richard PLANTAGENET, Duke of YORK was born in Shrewsbury 17 AUG 1473. Richard died about 1483. He married Anne MOWBRAY in St. Stephen's Chapel, Westminster, 15 JAN 1478. Anne died 19 NOV 1481 in Greenwich.

198 vii. Anne PLANTAGENET was born in Palace of Westminster 2 NOV 1475. Anne died 23 NOV 1511 at 36 years of age. She married Thomas HOWARD, 3rd Duke of NORFOLK 4 FEB 1495. Thomas is the son of Thomas HOWARD 2nd Duke of NORFOLK.

199 viii. George PLANTAGENET, Duke of BEDFORD was born in Windsor Castle MAR 1477. George died MAR 1479 in Windsor Castle, at 2 years of age.

200 ix. Catherine PLANTAGENET was born in Eltham Palace 14 AUG 1479. Catherine died 15 NOV 1527 in Tiverton, Devon, at 48 years of age. She married William COURTENAY, Earl of DEVON before OCT 1495.

201 x. Bridget PLANTAGENET(116) was born in Eltham Palace 10 NOV 1480. Bridget died 1517 in Dartford, at 36 years of age. became a nun at Dartford
