202. Elizabeth19 WOODVILLE (Sir_Richard20 WOODVILLE, 1st Earl RIVERS)(117) was born in Grafton Regis, Northants about 1437. Elizabeth died 8 JUN 1492 in Bermondsey Abbey, at 54 years of age. Her body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor.

She married twice. She married Sir_John GREY. Sir_John died 17 FEB 1461.(118) She married EDWARD IV PLANTAGENET, Earl of MARCH in Grafton Regis, 1 MAY 1464. EDWARD was born in Rouen, France 28 APR 1442. EDWARD(119) was the son of Richard PLANTAGENET Duke of YORK and Cicely NEVILLE. EDWARD died 9 APR 1483 in Palace of Westminster, at 40 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor Castle. Married Edward IV in secret.

Elizabeth WOODVILLE and Sir_John GREY had the following child:
