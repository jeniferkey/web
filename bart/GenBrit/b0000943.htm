1262. Ermentrude37 of ALSACE (Luitfrid III38) birth date unknown.

She married Conrad II of STRATLINGEN. Conrad was born 825. Conrad(973) was the son of Conrad I of ALTORF & BURGUNDY and Adela (Aelis) of TOURS & ALSACE. Conrad died 881(79,80). (See Conrad II of STRATLINGEN for the children resulting from this marriage.)
