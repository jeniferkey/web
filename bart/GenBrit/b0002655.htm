1018. Emma33 FITZOSBERN (William34, Osbern35 , Herfast36 deCREPON) birth date unknown.

She married Ralph deGAEL deMONTFORT, Earl of NORFOLK & SUFFOLK. Ralph was born about 1040. Ralph(744) was the son of Ralph Earl of NORFOLK & SUFFOLK and Emma of IRVY. Ralph died about 1096.(745) (See Ralph deGAEL deMONTFORT, Earl of NORFOLK & SUFFOLK for the children resulting from this marriage.)
