36. Alexandra6 of Denmark (Christian IX6) was born in Yellow Palace, Copenhagen, Denmark 1 DEC 1844. Alexandra died 20 NOV 1925 in Sandringham House, Norfolk, England, at 80 years of age. Her body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor.

She married EDWARD VII Albert Guelph SAXE-COBURG in St.George's Chap, Windsor, Berks., England, 10 MAR 1863. EDWARD was born in Buckingham Pal., London, England 9 NOV 1841. EDWARD was the son of Albert Augustus of SAXE-COBURG-GOTHA and Alexandrina VICTORIA of SAXE-COBURG-SAALFELD. EDWARD died 6 MAY 1910 in Buckingham Palace, at 68 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor. (See EDWARD VII Albert Guelph SAXE-COBURG for the children resulting from this marriage.)
