228. Jacquetta20 of Luxembourg(146) birth date unknown.

She married twice. She married Sir_Richard WOODVILLE, 1st Earl RIVERS. (Additional notes for Sir_Richard WOODVILLE, 1st Earl RIVERS(147)) Sir_Richard died 1469 in Kenilworth.(148) She married John PLANTAGENET, Duke of BEDFORD in Therouenne, 22 APR 1433. John was born 20 JUN 1389. John(149) was the son of HENRY IV BOLINGBROKE Earl of DERBY and Mary deBOHUN. John died 15 SEP 1435 in Rouen, at 46 years of age. Fined 1,000 Pounds after wedding Sir Richard Woodville for marrying beneath her station.
