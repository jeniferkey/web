117. GEORGE I Louis Guelph11 HANOVER (Ernest Augustus12 Duke of BRUNSWICK-LUNEBURG) was born in Osnabruck, Brunswick, Germany 28 MAY 1660.(46) RESEARCH NOTES: Also: Leineschloss, Hanover Individual flags: KingGrBr. GEORGE died 11 JUN 1727 in Osnabruck, Brunswick, Germany, at 67 years of age.(47) RESEARCH NOTES: Suffered a cerebral hemorrhage as he was travelling by coach to Osnabruck; he was taken to that castle and died in the very room in which he had been born. His body was interred in Leineschloss Church, Hanover.(48) RESEARCH NOTES: Removed to Herrenhausen in 1957 following damage to tomb in WWII.

He married Sophia Dorothea of CELLE (ZELL) in Zell, Germany, 21 NOV 1682. Sophia was born in Zell, Germany 3 FEB 1666. Sophia was the daughter of George William Duke of BRUNSWICK-LUNEBURG-CELLE and Eleonore Desmiers d'OLBREUSE. Sophia died 3 NOV 1726 in Ahlden, Germany, at 60 years of age.(49) Her body was interred in Zell, Germany.
