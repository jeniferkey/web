16. Elizabeth Angela Marguerite4 BOWES-LYON (Claude George5) was born in St.Paul's, Waldenbury, England 4 AUG 1900.

She married GEORGE VI Albert Frederick Arthur WINDSOR in Westminster Abbey, 26 APR 1923. GEORGE was born in York Cottage, Sandringham, Norfolk, England 14 DEC 1895. GEORGE was the son of GEORGE V Albert Guelph WINDSOR Duke of YORK and Mary Victoria Augusta of TECK. GEORGE died 6 FEB 1952 in Sandringham, Norfolk, England, at 56 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor. (See GEORGE VI Albert Frederick Arthur WINDSOR for the children resulting from this marriage.)
