591. Geoffrey V29 PLANTAGENET of ANJOU (FULK V "The Young"30 of ANJOU, Fulk IV "Rechin"31, Aubri-Geoffrey32 of the GATINAIS, Geoffrey III33)(397) was born 23 AUG 1113. Geoffrey died 7 SEP 1151 in Chateau-du-Loire, at 38 years of age.

He married twice. He married unknown.(398) RESEARCH NOTES: mistress
