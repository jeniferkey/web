1257. Ermentrude37 of Orleans (Odo38) birth date unknown. Ermentrude died 6 OCT 869.

She married CHARLES "The Bald" 14 DEC 842. CHARLES was born in Frankfort-on-Maine 13 JUN 828. CHARLES(968) was the son of LOUIS I "The Pious" "The Fair" and Judith of Bavaria. CHARLES died 6 OCT 877 at 49 years of age. (See CHARLES "The Bald" for the children resulting from this marriage.)
