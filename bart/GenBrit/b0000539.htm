Elfgifu33 (ETHELRED II "The Unready"33, EDGAR "The Peaceful"34, EDMUND I "The Magnificent"35, EDWARD I "The Elder"36, ALFRED "The Great"37, ETHELWULF ("Noble Wolf")38, EGBERT39, EAHLMUND40, EAFA41, EOPPA42, INGILD43, CENRED44, CEOWALD45, CUTHA46, CUTHWINE47, (Ceolin)48 CAEELIN, CYNRIC49 , CERDIC50) birth date unknown.

She married twice. She married Aelfgar III MALET of MERCIA. Aelfgar was born about 1031. Aelfgar(730) was the son of Leofric of MERCIA and Godgifu (Godiva). Aelfgar died CA 1062(59). She married Uchtred MacSIGURD, Earl of NORTHUMBERLAND. Uchtred was the son of Sigurd (Siward) MacMALDRED Earl of HUNTINGDON and Aelfled of BERNICIA. Uchtred died 1016.(731)

Uchtred MacSIGURD, Earl of NORTHUMBERLAND and Elfgifu had the following child:

999 i. Algifu32.
