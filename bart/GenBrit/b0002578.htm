1264. Elfleda37 birth date unknown.

She married EDWARD I "The Elder". EDWARD was born 875. EDWARD(974) was the son of ALFRED "The Great" and Ealhswith. EDWARD died 17 JUL 924(25) in Farndon-on-Dee, Mercia. His body was interred in Winchester. (See EDWARD I "The Elder" for the children resulting from this marriage.)
