72. GEORGE III8 HANOVER (Frederick Louis9 HANOVER, Prince of Wales, GEORGE II Augustus Guelph10 HANOVER, GEORGE I Louis Guelph11, Ernest Augustus12 Duke of BRUNSWICK-LUNEBURG) was born in Norfolk House, St. James' Square, London 4 JUN 1738. Individual flags: KingGrBr. GEORGE died 29 JAN 1820 in Windsor Castle, at 81 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor.

He married Charlotte Sophia of MECKLENBERG-STRELITZ in St. James' Palace, 8 SEP 1761. Charlotte was born in Mirow 19 MAY 1744. Charlotte was the daughter of Charles Louis Frederick and Elizabeth Albertine. Charlotte died 17 NOV 1818 in Kew Palace, at 74 years of age. Her body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor.

GEORGE III HANOVER and Charlotte Sophia of MECKLENBERG-STRELITZ had the following children:

73 i. GEORGE IV7 HANOVER(33) was born in St. James's Palace 12 AUG 1762. Individual flags: KingGrBr. GEORGE died 26 JUN 1830 in Windsor Castle, at 67 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor. He married Caroline Amelia Elizabeth BRUNSWICK-WOLFENBUTTEL in Chapel Royal, St. James's Palace, 8 APR 1795. Caroline was born in Brunswick 17 MAY 1768. Caroline was the daughter of Charles II Duke of BRUNSWICK-WOLFENBUTTEL and Augusta HANOVER. Caroline died 7 AUG 1821 in Brandenburg House, Hammersmith, at 53 years of age. Her body was interred in Brunswick. Prince Regent from 5 FEB 1811
