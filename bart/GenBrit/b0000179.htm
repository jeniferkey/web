690. Adeliza30 leGRANTMESNIL (Hugh31, Robert32, Hugh33 leGRANTMESNIL, Count of GRANTMESNIL) birth date unknown. Adeliza died after 1107.

She married Roger leBIGOD before 1103. Roger(483) was the son of Robert leBIGOD and unknown. Roger died 8 SEP 1107. (See Roger leBIGOD for the children resulting from this marriage.)
