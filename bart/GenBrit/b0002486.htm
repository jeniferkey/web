246. Margaret21 HOLLAND birth date unknown. Margaret died 31 DEC 1439.

She married twice. She married John BEAUFORT, Marquess of DORSET. John was born about 1373. John(165) was the son of John of Gaunt PLANTAGENET Duke of LANCASTER and Catherine SWYNFORD. John died 1410 at 37 years of age. She married Thomas PLANTAGENET, Duke of CLARENCE 1412. Thomas was born in Kenilworth 1388. Thomas was the son of HENRY IV BOLINGBROKE Earl of DERBY and Mary deBOHUN. Thomas died 22 MAR 1421 at 32 years of age.(166)
