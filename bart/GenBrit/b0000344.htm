683. Ranulf "de Briquessart"30 deMESCHIN, 1st Earl of CHESTER (Ranulf31 deMESCHIN, Vicomte de BAYEUX, Ranulf32 Comte de BAYEUX)(480) was born before 1066. Ranulf died CA 1128(29). His body was interred in St. Werburg's, Chester, England.

He married Lucia (Lucy) TAILLEBOIS about 1098. Lucia was the daughter of Ivo TAILLEBOIS and Lucia MALET. Lucia died about 1136. Vicomte de Bayeux, Earl of Chester (1120)
