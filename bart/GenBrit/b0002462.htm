323. Edmund23 MORTIMER, Earl of MARCH (Roger24 MORTIMER of WIGMORE, Edmund25 MORTIMER, Roger26, Edmund27, Roger28, Ralph29) birth date unknown. Edmund died 1381.

He married Philippa PLANTAGENET. Philippa(211) was the daughter of Lionel PLANTAGENET of ANTWERP and Elizabeth deBURGH of ULSTER. Philippa died 1382.

Edmund MORTIMER, Earl of MARCH and Philippa PLANTAGENET had the following children:

+ 285 i. Roger22 MORTIMER, Earl of MARCH.

324 ii. Edmund MORTIMER. He married Catrin GLYNDWR. Catrin is the daughter of Owain GLYN DWR and unknown HANMER.

325 iii. unknown MORTIMER. She married Henry "Hotspur" PERCY, Earl of NORTHUMBERLAND. Henry died 21 JUL 1403 in Shrewsbury.(212)
