114. Caroline10 of ANSPACH (John Frederick11 ) was born in Anspach, Germany 1 MAR 1682. Caroline died 20 NOV 1737 in St.James' Palace, London, England, at 55 years of age. Her body was interred in Westminster Abbey.

She married GEORGE II Augustus Guelph HANOVER in Herrenhausen, 22 AUG 1705. GEORGE was born in Herrenhausen, Hanover, Germany 30 OCT 1683. GEORGE(44) was the son of GEORGE I Louis Guelph HANOVER and Sophia Dorothea of CELLE (ZELL). GEORGE died 25 OCT 1760 in Kensington Pal., London, England, at 76 years of age.(45) His body was interred in Westminster Abbey. (See GEORGE II Augustus Guelph HANOVER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
