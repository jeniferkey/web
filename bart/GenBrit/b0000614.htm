922. Richard II "Le Bon"33 4th Duke of Normandy (Richard I "The Fearless"34 3rd Duke of Normandy, William35 ) was born 996. Richard died 28 AUG 1026.

He married twice. He married Judith CA 1000(08). Judith was born 982. Judith(668) was the daughter of Conan "Le Tort" of Brittany and Ermengarde of ANJOU. Judith died 8) 1017. He married Papia d'ENVERMEU.

Richard II "Le Bon" 4th Duke of Normandy and Judith had the following children:

+ 813 i. Robert II "Le Diable"32 6th Duke of Normandy.

+ 923 ii. Papia.

+ 924 iii. Alice.

+ 925 iv. Richard III 5th Duke of Normandy.

926 v. Mauger "The Younger"(669). He married Germaine of CORBEIL. (Additional notes for Germaine of CORBEIL(670)) Archbp. of Rouen, 1037-1054; Lord of St. Clair in Manche

927 vi. Eleanor. She married Baldwin IV "The Bearded" of Flanders. Baldwin was born 980. Baldwin(671) was the son of (Arnold) Arnulf II "The Young" Count of Flanders and Rosele (Susanna). Baldwin died 30 MAY 1035. His body was interred in Chapel of St, Laurent, Abbey of St.Pierre.

928 vii. William(672). Count of Arques; dispossessed 1053
