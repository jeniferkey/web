41. Ludwig IV6 Grand Duke of HESSE and RHINE (Charles7 )(23) was born 1837. Ludwig died 1892 at 55 years of age.

He married Alice Maud Mary SAXE-COBURG in Osborne House, Isle of Wight, 1 JUL 1862. Alice was born in Buckingham Palace 25 APR 1843. Alice was the daughter of Albert Augustus of SAXE-COBURG-GOTHA and Alexandrina VICTORIA of SAXE-COBURG-SAALFELD. Alice died 15 DEC 1878 in Darmstadt, at 35 years of age. AKA "Louis of Hesse-Darmstadt"
