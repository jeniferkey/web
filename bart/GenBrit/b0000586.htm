628. Bertrade29 deMONTFORT (Simon30 deMONTFORT, Count d'EVREUX, Amauri31, Simon32 deMONTFORT, Amauri33, WILLIAM34 of HAINAULT) was born 1155. Bertrade died 1227 at 72 years of age.

She married Hugh deGUERNAN, 3rd Earl of CHESTER 1169. Hugh was born 1147. Hugh(430) was the son of Ranulf "de Briquessard" deGUERNAN 2nd Earl of CHES and Lady_Maud deCAEN of GLOUCESTER. Hugh died 1181 in Leeke Co, Stafford, England, at 34 years of age. (See Hugh deGUERNAN, 3rd Earl of CHESTER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
