Gisela38 of France (LOUIS I "The Pious" "The Fair"38 , CHARLEMAGNE39, PEPIN "The Short"40, Charles41 MARTEL, Pepin II "The Younger"42 of HERISTAL, Ansgise (Ansegis)43 , Arnulf44, Bodegisel II45 of METZ, Bodegisel I46 , Munderic47 of VITRY-EN-PERTHOIS, CLODERIC "The Parricide"48 , SIGEBERT "The Lame"49, CHILDEBERT50 of COLOGNE, CLOVIS "The Riparian"51) was born 820. Gisela died 1 JUL 874.

She married Eberhard Count of BURGUNDY. Eberhard was the son of Henroch (Hunrock) of FRIEUL and Engeltron. Eberhard died about 864. (See Eberhard Count of BURGUNDY for the children resulting from this marriage.)
