She married twice. She married Robert deBEAUMONT, 1st Earl of LEICESTER. Robert was born about 1049. Robert(393) was the son of Roger deBEAUMONT and Adelise (Avelina) deMEULAN. Robert died 5 JUN 1118 at 68 years of age. She married William deWARENNE, 2nd Earl of SURREY 1118. William was the son of William deWARENNE 1st Earl of SURREY and Gundred "The Fleming of Normandy". William died 11 MAY 1138.
