380. Eleanor25 of CASTILE (Ferdinand III26) was born in Castile about 1244. Eleanor died 24 NOV 1290 in Herdeby, near Grantham, Lincolnshire, at 46 years of age. Her body was interred in Westminster Abbey.(254) RESEARCH NOTES: Her heart is kept at Blackfriars Church.

She married EDWARD I "Longshanks" PLANTAGENET in Las Huelgas, OCT 1254. EDWARD was born in Palace of Westminster 17 JUN 1239. EDWARD(255) was the son of HENRY III PLANTAGENET and Eleanor of PROVENCE. EDWARD died 7 JUL 1307 in Burgh-on-the-Sands, near Carlisle, at 68 years of age. His body was interred in Westminster Abbey. (See EDWARD I "Longshanks" PLANTAGENET for the children resulting from this marriage.)
