209. Isabel20 SIBBALD of BALGONY birth date unknown.

She married George DOUGLAS, 4th Earl of ANGUS before 1446. George was the son of William DOUGLAS 2nd Earl of ANGUS and Margaret HAY of YESTER. George died 12 MAR 1463. (See George DOUGLAS, 4th Earl of ANGUS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
