645. Matilda (Edith)30 (MALCOLM III "Canmore"30, DUNCAN I31 MacCRINAN, CRINAN "The Thane"32 ) was born in Dunfermline about 1079. Matilda died 1 MAY 1118 in Palace of Westminster, at 38 years of age. Her body was interred in Westminster Abbey.

She married HENRY I "The Lion of Justice" in Westminster Abbey, 11 NOV 1100. HENRY was born in Selby, Yorkshire SEP 1068. HENRY(462) was the son of WILLIAM I "The Conqueror" 7th Duke of Normandy and Matilda (Maud) of Flanders. HENRY died 1 DEC 1135 in St. Denis-le-Fermont, near Gisors, at 67 years of age.(463) His body was interred in Reading Abbey. (See HENRY I "The Lion of Justice" for the children resulting from this marriage.)
