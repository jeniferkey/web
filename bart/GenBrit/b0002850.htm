308. Thomas23 apLLYWELYN (Llywelyn24 abOWAIN, Owain25 apMAREDUDD, Maredudd26 abOWAIN, Owain27 apGRUFFUDD, Gruffudd28 apRHYS, Rhys "The Lord"29 apGRUFFUDD) birth date unknown.

He married an unknown person.

Thomas apLLYWELYN had the following children:

309 i. Elen22. She married Gruffudd Fychan II apMADOG. Gruffudd Fychan II was the son of Madog apGRUFFUDD, of the primary line remaining of Powys. The son of Elen and Gruffudd Fychan II was Owain Glyn Dwr (Owen Glendower), whose rebellion was the last serious Welsh threat to English power.

+ 272 ii. Margaret.
