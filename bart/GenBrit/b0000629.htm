753. Matilda (Maud)31 of Flanders (Baldwin V "De Lilles"32, Baldwin IV "The Bearded"33, (Arnold) Arnulf II "The Young"34 Count of Flanders, (Baudoin) Baldwin III35, Arnulf I (Arnold) "The Old"36 Count of Flanders & ARTOIS, Baldwin II37 Count of Flanders, ARTOIS & BOULOGNE, Baldwin I "Bras-de-Fer"38 of Flanders) was born in Flanders about 1031. Matilda died 3 NOV 1083 in Caen, at 52 years of age. Her body was interred in Abbey of the Holy Trinity, Caen.

She married WILLIAM I "The Conqueror" 7th Duke of Normandy about 1053. WILLIAM was born in Falaise, Normandy 8) 1027. WILLIAM(543) was the son of Robert II "Le Diable" 6th Duke of Normandy and Herleve (Arlette). WILLIAM died 9 SEP 1087 in Priory of St. Gervaise near Ro. His body was interred in Abbey of St. Stephen, Caen. (See WILLIAM I "The Conqueror" 7th Duke of Normandy for the children resulting from this marriage.)
