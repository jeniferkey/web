667. GEZA II30 ARPAD, King of Hungary (GEZA I31)(464) was born abt 1130. GEZA died 31 MAY 1162.

He married Euphrosyne Monomakh. Euphrosyne was born in Kiev, Russia abt 1130. Euphrosyne is the daughter of Vladimir II Monomakh and Gytha Haroldson. King of Hungary, 1141-1162

GEZA II ARPAD, King of Hungary and Euphrosyne Monomakh had the following child:

+ 599 i. BELA III29 ARPAD, King of Hungary.
