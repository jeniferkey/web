310. EDWARD III23 PLANTAGENET (EDWARD II24, EDWARD I "Longshanks"25, HENRY III26, JOHN "Lackland"27, HENRY II28, Geoffrey V29 PLANTAGENET of ANJOU, FULK V "The Young"30 of ANJOU, Fulk IV "Rechin"31, Aubri-Geoffrey32 of the GATINAIS, Geoffrey III33)(205) was born in Windsor Castle 13 NOV 1312. EDWARD died 21 JUN 1377 in Sheen Palace, at 64 years of age. His body was interred in Westminster Abbey.

He married Philippa of HAINAULT in York Minster, 24 JAN 1328. Philippa was born in Valenciennes 24 JUN 1311. Philippa was the daughter of Willem III Count of HAINAULT and Jeanne CAPET of VALOIS. Philippa died 14 AUG 1369 in Windsor Castle, at 58 years of age.(206) Her body was interred in Westminster Abbey.

He was christened in Chapel of St. Edward, Windsor, 17 NOV 1312. Keeper of the Realm from 26 OCT 1326; King of England 1327-1377.
