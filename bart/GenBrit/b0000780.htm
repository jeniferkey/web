1246. Elfthryth37 (ALFRED "The Great"37, ETHELWULF ("Noble Wolf")38, EGBERT39, EAHLMUND40, EAFA41, EOPPA42, INGILD43, CENRED44, CEOWALD45, CUTHA46, CUTHWINE47, (Ceolin)48 CAEELIN, CYNRIC49 , CERDIC50) birth date unknown. Elfthryth died 7 JUN 929.

She married Baldwin II Count of Flanders, ARTOIS & BOULOGNE 884. Baldwin was born about 865. Baldwin was the son of Baldwin I "Bras-de-Fer" of Flanders and Judith of France. Baldwin died 2 JAN 918(19). His body was interred in Abbey of Stes. Peter & Paul. (See Baldwin II Count of Flanders, ARTOIS & BOULOGNE for the children resulting from this marriage.)
