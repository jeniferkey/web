1388. Edith (Hedwig)39 of SAXONY birth date unknown.

She married Guelph (Welf) I of Bavaria. Guelph was born 787. Guelph(1073) was the son of Isenbart of ALTORF and Ermentrude (Irmentrudis) of Swabia. Guelph died CA 818(820). (See Guelph (Welf) I of Bavaria for the children resulting from this marriage.)
