43. Alice Maud Mary6 SAXE-COBURG (Albert Augustus7 of SAXE-COBURG-GOTHA, Ernest I8 ) was born in Buckingham Palace 25 APR 1843. Alice died 15 DEC 1878 in Darmstadt, at 35 years of age.

She married Ludwig IV Grand Duke of HESSE and RHINE in Osborne House, Isle of Wight, 1 JUL 1862. Ludwig was born 1837. Ludwig(25) was the son of Charles and Elizabeth. Ludwig died 1892 at 55 years of age. (See Ludwig IV Grand Duke of HESSE and RHINE for the children resulting from this marriage.)
