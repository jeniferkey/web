MARY I15 STUART, Queen of Scots (JAMES V16 STEWART, King of Scots, Margaret17 TUDOR, Princess of England, HENRY VII18 TUDOR, Earl of RICHMOND, Edmund19, Owain20 TUDOR, Maredudd21 apTUDUR, Tudur22 apGORONWY, Goronwy23 apTUDUR, Tudur24 apGORONWY, Goronwy25 apEDNYFED, Ednyfed Fychan26 ) was born in Linlithgow Palace, Scotland 8 DEC 1542. Individual flags: KingScot. MARY died 8 FEB 1586 in Fotheringhay, Northants., England, at 43 years of age.(69) RESEARCH NOTES: Beheaded at the wish of England's queen, Elizabeth, after the discovery of the imprisoned Mary's "Casket Letter."

She married three times. She married James Hepburn. (Additional notes for James Hepburn(70)) James died 1578 in Norway.(71) She married FRANCIS II in Notre Dame, Paris, APR 1558. FRANCIS was born 1542. (Additional notes for FRANCIS II(72)) FRANCIS died DEC 1560 at 18 years of age. She married Henry STEWART, Lord DARNLEY in Chapel Royal, Holyrood House, 29 JUL 1565. Henry was born 7 DEC 1545. Henry(73) was the son of Matthew STEWART 13th Earl of LENNOX and Lady_Margaret DOUGLAS of ANGUS. Henry died 10 FEB 1566/1567 in Kirk o'Fields, Edinburgh, Scotland, at 21 years of age.(74)
