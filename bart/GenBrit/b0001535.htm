157. Elizabeth18 HAMILTON (James19 HAMILTON, 1st Lord HAMILTON) birth date unknown.

She married Matthew STEWART, 11th Earl of LENNOX. Matthew was the son of John STUART 10th Earl of LENNOX and Margaret MONTGOMERIE. Matthew died 9 SEP 1513.(85) (See Matthew STEWART, 11th Earl of LENNOX for the children resulting from this marriage.)
