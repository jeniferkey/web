790. Robert31 FITZHAMMO, Earl of GLOUCESTER(567) birth date unknown. Robert died 1105.

He married Sybil deMONTGOMERY. Sybil is the daughter of Roger deMONTGOMERY 1st Earl of ARUNDEL and Mabel d'ALENCON deTALVAS. Led attack which transferred Welsh territory of Glamorgan into Norman hands.

Robert FITZHAMMO, Earl of GLOUCESTER and Sybil deMONTGOMERY had the following child:

+ 710 i. Maud (Mabel)30 FITZHAMMO of GLOUCESTER.
