612. Juliana29 deVERE (Alberic30 deVERE, 2nd Baron deVERE, Alberic "Sanglie"31 deVERE, Count AUBREY, Alphonso32 deVERE, Count de GHESNES) birth date unknown.

She married Hugh leBIGOD. Hugh was born about 1095. Hugh(414) was the son of Roger leBIGOD and Adeliza leGRANTMESNIL. Hugh died 6) 1177. (See Hugh leBIGOD for the children resulting from this marriage.)
