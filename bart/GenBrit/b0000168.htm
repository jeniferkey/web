631. Hawise (Hawice)29 deBEAUMONT (Robert30, Robert "Robin the Hunchback"31 deBEAUMONT, 2nd Earl of LEICESTER) birth date unknown. Hawise died 24 APR 1197.

She married William FITZROBERT, 2nd Earl of GLOUCESTER about 1150. William(432) was the son of Robert "The Counsel" deCAEN 1st Earl of GLOUCESTER and Maud (Mabel) FITZHAMMO of GLOUCESTER. William died 23 NOV 1183. (See William FITZROBERT, 2nd Earl of GLOUCESTER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
