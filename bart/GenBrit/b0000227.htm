477. Margaret (Margery)27 deBRIOUSE (William28, William29, Philip30, Guillaume31) birth date unknown. Margaret died 19 NOV 1200.

She married WALTER deLACY. WALTER was born about 1172. WALTER(317) was the son of Hugh deLACY and unknown. WALTER died 1241 at 69 years of age. (See WALTER deLACY for the children resulting from this marriage.)
