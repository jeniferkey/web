392. Avelina (FitzJohn)25 FITZGEOFFERY (Sir_John26 FITZGEOFFREY, Geoffrey27 FITZPIERS, Earl of ESSEX) birth date unknown. Avelina died about 20 MAY 1274. Her body was interred in Dunmow Priory.

She married Walter deBURGH about 1257. Walter was the son of Richard deBURGH and Egidia deLACY. Walter died 28 JUL 1271. (See Walter deBURGH for the children resulting from this marriage.)
