835. Ermengarde32 of ANJOU (Fulk III "The Bearded"33, Geoffrey I "Grisgonelle"34, Fulk II "The Good"35) birth date unknown. Ermengarde died 21 MAR 1076.

She married twice. She married Conan "Le Tort" of Brittany 980. (Additional notes for Conan "Le Tort" of Brittany(608)) Conan died 27 JUN 992.(609) She married Aubri-Geoffrey of the GATINAIS about 1035. Aubri-Geoffrey(610) was the son of Geoffrey III of the GATINAIS and Beatrix of MACON. Aubri-Geoffrey died 1 APR 1046.
