225. Sir_Richard20 WOODVILLE, 1st Earl RIVERS(141) birth date unknown. Sir_Richard died 1469 in Kenilworth.(142) SOURCE NOTES: _The Birth of Britain_, Winston S. Churchill, p.340.RESEARCH NOTES: Executed by command of Warwick, the King-Maker, who at this time held both Henry VI and Edward IV prisoner, one in the Tower and the other at Middleham.

He married Jacquetta of Luxembourg. (Additional notes for Jacquetta of Luxembourg(143)) Sir Richard Woodville had been steward to John, Duke of Bedford, and, some time after that noble had died, married his widow Jacquetta.

Sir_Richard WOODVILLE, 1st Earl RIVERS and Jacquetta of Luxembourg had the following children:

226 i. Anthony19 WOODVILLE.

+ 202 ii. Elizabeth WOODVILLE was born about 1437.

227 iii. John WOODVILLE(144) was born about 1446. John died 1469 in Kenilworth, at 23 years of age.(145) RESEARCH NOTES: Executed along with his father, Earl Rivers. He married Catherine Duchess of NORFOLK.

Catherine was born about 1385. SOURCE NOTES: _The Lives of the Kings and Queens of England_; Antonia Fraser, ed.; p.152. Among the many marriages arranges for relatives of Elizabeth's was this one for her brother, John. As one chronicler put it: "Catherine, Duchess of Norfolk, a slip of a girl about eighty years old, was married to John Woodville, aged twenty years. A diabolical marriage."
