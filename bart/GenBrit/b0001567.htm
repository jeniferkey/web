173. Margaret18 of Denmark(98) birth date unknown. Margaret died 14 JUL 1486.

She married JAMES III STEWART, King of Scots 13 JUL 1469. JAMES was born 10 JUL 1451. JAMES was the son of JAMES II STEWART King of Scots and Mary deGUELDRES. JAMES died 11 JUN 1488 in Sauchieburn, at 36 years of age.(99) His body was interred in Cambuskenneth. (See JAMES III STEWART, King of Scots for the children resulting from this marriage.) Princess
