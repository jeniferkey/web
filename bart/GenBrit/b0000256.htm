ROBERT I24 BRUCE, King of Scots (Robert25 BRUCE of CLEVELAND & ANNANDALE, Robert "The Competitor"26 BRUCE, 5th Earl of ANNANDAL, Robert27 BRUCE, 4th Lord of ANNANDALE, William28 BRUCE, Robert29 BRUCE, 2nd Lord of ANNANDALE, Robert30 BRUCE, 1st Lord of ANNANDALE)(240) was born in Writtle, Essex, England 27 MAR 1274 (or 11 JUL 1274). Individual flags: KingScot. ROBERT died 7 JUN 1329 in Cardross near Dumbarton, Scotland. His body was interred in Dunfermline, Scotland.

He married twice. He married Isabel (Matilda) of MAR about 1295. Isabel(241) was the daughter of (David) Donald macWILLIAM Earl of MAR. Isabel died 1297. He married Elizabeth deBURGH 1302. Elizabeth was the daughter of Richard deBURGH Earl of ULSTER and Margaret deBURGH. Elizabeth died 26 OCT 1327. King of Scotland (after considerable effort)

ROBERT I BRUCE, King of Scots and Elizabeth deBURGH had the following child:

354 i. DAVID II23 BRUCE, King of Scots(242). Individual flags: KingScot. He married Joan PLANTAGENET. Joan was born in Woodstock about FEB 1335. Joan was the daughter of EDWARD III PLANTAGENET and Philippa of HAINAULT. Joan died 2 SEP 1348 in Bayonne, at 13 years of age. Taken prisoner by armies of the fighting Archbishop of York, William de la Zouche, at Neville's Cross near Durham.
