1223. NEST36 of POWYS (Cadell37 apBROCHWEL, Brochwel38 )(939) birth date unknown.

She married MERFYN FRYCH "The Freckled". MERFYN was the son of GWRIAD of MANAU and ESYLLT of GWYNEDD. MERFYN died 844. (See MERFYN FRYCH "The Freckled" for the children resulting from this marriage.) Queen of Powys
