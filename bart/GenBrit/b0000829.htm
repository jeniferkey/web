Judith38 of France (CHARLES "The Bald"37 , LOUIS I "The Pious" "The Fair"38, CHARLEMAGNE39, PEPIN "The Short"40, Charles41 MARTEL, Pepin II "The Younger"42 of HERISTAL, Ansgise (Ansegis)43 , Arnulf44, Bodegisel II45 of METZ, Bodegisel I46 , Munderic47 of VITRY-EN-PERTHOIS, CLODERIC "The Parricide"48 , SIGEBERT "The Lame"49, CHILDEBERT50 of COLOGNE, CLOVIS "The Riparian"51)(1055) was born 846.

She married three times. She married Baldwin I "Bras-de-Fer" of Flanders 862. (Additional notes for Baldwin I "Bras-de-Fer" of Flanders(1056)) Baldwin died 879. She married ETHELWULF ("Noble Wolf") in Verberie-sur-Oise, 1 OCT 856. ETHELWULF(1057) was the son of EGBERT and Raedburgh (Redburga). ETHELWULF died 13 JAN 858. His body was interred in Steyning, Sussex (moved to Winchester). She married ETHELBALD after 858. ETHELBALD(1058) was the son of ETHELWULF ("Noble Wolf") and Osburgh (Osburga). ETHELBALD died 860. His body was interred in Sherborne Abbey. marriage was annulled by the Church as incestuous
