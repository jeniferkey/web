64. Alexandrina VICTORIA7 of SAXE-COBURG-SAALFELD (Edward Augustus Guelph8 HANOVER, Duke of KENT) was born in Kensington, London, England 24 MAY 1819. Individual flags: KingGrBr. Alexandrina died 22 JAN 1901 in Osborne House, Isle of Wight, England, at 81 years of age. Her body was interred in Frogmore Palace, Windsor, Berks., England.

She married Albert Augustus of SAXE-COBURG-GOTHA in Chapel Royal, St. James' Palace, London, England, 10 FEB 1840. Albert was born in Schloss Rosenau, near Coburg, Germany 28 AUG 1819. Albert(30) was the son of Ernest I and Louise. Albert died 14 DEC 1861 in Windsor Castle, Berks., England, at 42 years of age. His body was interred in Frogmore, Windsor, Berks., England. (See Albert Augustus of SAXE-COBURG-GOTHA for the children resulting from this marriage.)
