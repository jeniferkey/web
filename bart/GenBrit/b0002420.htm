315. Lionel24 PLANTAGENET of ANTWERP (EDWARD III23 PLANTAGENET, EDWARD II24, EDWARD I "Longshanks"25, HENRY III26, JOHN "Lackland"27, HENRY II28, Geoffrey V29 PLANTAGENET of ANJOU, FULK V "The Young"30 of ANJOU, Fulk IV "Rechin"31, Aubri-Geoffrey32 of the GATINAIS, Geoffrey III33)(236) was born in Antwerp 29 NOV 1338. Lionel died 17 OCT 1368 in Alba, Piedmont, at 29 years of age.

He married twice. He married Elizabeth deBURGH of ULSTER in Tower of London, 9 SEP 1342. (Additional notes for Elizabeth deBURGH of ULSTER(237)) Elizabeth died 10 DEC 1363 in Dublin. He married Violante VISCONTI of Milan in Milan, 28 MAY 1368. Violante died 1404. Duke of Clarence
