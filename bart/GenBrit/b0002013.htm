Elizabeth18 PLANTAGENET of YORK (EDWARD IV19 PLANTAGENET, Earl of MARCH, Richard20 PLANTAGENET, Duke of YORK, Richard21 PLANTAGENET, Earl of CAMBRIDGE, Edmund22 PLANTAGENET of LANGLEY, Duke of YORK, EDWARD III23 PLANTAGENET, EDWARD II24, EDWARD I "Longshanks"25, HENRY III26, JOHN "Lackland"27, HENRY II28, Geoffrey V29 PLANTAGENET of ANJOU, FULK V "The Young"30 of ANJOU, Fulk IV "Rechin"31, Aubri-Geoffrey32 of the GATINAIS, Geoffrey III33) was born in Palace of Westminster 11 FEB 1466. Elizabeth died 11 FEB 1503 in Tower of London, at 37 years of age. Her body was interred in King Henry VII's Chapel, Westminster Abbey.

She married HENRY VII TUDOR, Earl of RICHMOND in Westminster, 18 JAN 1486. HENRY was born in Pembroke Castle 28 JAN 1457. HENRY(95) was the son of Edmund TUDOR Earl of RICHMOND and Margaret BEAUFORT. HENRY died 21 APR 1509 in Richmond Palace, at 52 years of age. His body was interred in King Henry VII's Chapel, Westminster Abbey. (See HENRY VII TUDOR, Earl of RICHMOND for the children resulting from this marriage.)
