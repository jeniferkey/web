955. Hildegarde33 birth date unknown. Hildegarde died 1 APR 1040 in Jerusalem.

She married Fulk III "The Bearded" of ANJOU after 1000. Fulk(697) was the son of Geoffrey I "Grisgonelle" of ANJOU and Adelaide deVERMANDOIS. Fulk died 21 JUN 1040. (See Fulk III "The Bearded" of ANJOU for the children resulting from this marriage.)
