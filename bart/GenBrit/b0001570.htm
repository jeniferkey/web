He married Lady_Margaret DOUGLAS of ANGUS 29 JUN 1544. Lady_Margaret was the daughter of Archibald DOUGLAS 6th Earl of ANGUS and Margaret TUDOR Princess of England. Lady_Margaret died 9 MAR 1577/1578. Regent of Scotland

Matthew STEWART, 13th Earl of LENNOX and Lady_Margaret DOUGLAS of ANGUS had the following children:

+ 138 i. Henry15 STEWART, Lord DARNLEY was born 7 DEC 1545.

143 ii. Charles STEWART EARL of LENNOX was born about 1556. Charles died about 1576. He married Elizabeth CAVENDISH about 1574. Elizabeth was the daughter of William CAVENDISH and Elizabeth (Bess) of HARDWICKE. Elizabeth died about JAN 1581/1582.
