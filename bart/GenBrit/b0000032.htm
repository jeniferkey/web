333. Sir_John24 STEWART of BONKYL (Alexander25 STEWART, 4th High Steward of Scotland, Walter26 STEWART, 3rd High Steward of Scotland, Alan27 FITZWALTER, 2nd High Steward of Scotland, Walter28 FITZALAN, 1st High Steward of Scotland, Alan29 FITZFLAALD, Lord OSWESTRY, Flaald30 FITZGUIENOC, Guienoc31 FITZHAMMO, HAMO32 )(217) birth date unknown. Sir_John died about 1298 in Falkirk.(218) RESEARCH NOTES: killed in battle

He married Margaret BONKYL. (Additional notes for Margaret BONKYL(219)) Attained Bonkyl through wife

Sir_John STEWART of BONKYL and Margaret BONKYL had the following children:

334 i. Sir_Alexander23 STEWART of BONKYL. Sir_Alexander died about 1319. He married an unknown person.

335 ii. Sir_Walter STEWART of GARLIES(220). He married an unknown person. Baron of Garlies & Dalswinton

336 iii. Sir_John STEWART of DALDAR. Sir_John died 1333 in Halidon Hill.(221) RESEARCH NOTES: killed in battle

337 iv. Lady_Isabel STEWART of GARLIES. She married Thomas FITZ RANDOLPH, Earl of MORAY. Thomas(222) was the son of Thomas RANDOLPH. Thomas died 20 JUL 1332.

+ 301 v. Sir_Alan STEWART of DREGHORN.
