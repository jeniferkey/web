352. Egidia24 deBURGH (Walter25, Richard26, William27) birth date unknown.

She married James STEWART, 5th High Steward of Scotland. James was born about 1243. James was the son of Alexander STEWART 4th High Steward of Scotland and Jean of the Isles. James died 16 JUL 1309 at 66 years of age. (See James STEWART, 5th High Steward of Scotland for the children resulting from this marriage.)
