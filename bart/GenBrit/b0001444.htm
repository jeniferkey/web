257. Annabella21 DRUMMOND birth date unknown. Annabella died about 1401.

She married ROBERT III (John) STEWART, King of Scots about 1367. ROBERT was born about 1337. ROBERT was the son of ROBERT II STEWART King of Scots and Elizabeth MURE (MUIR). ROBERT died 4 APR 1406 in Dundonald, Scotland, at 68 years of age.(175) (See ROBERT III (John) STEWART, King of Scots for the children resulting from this marriage.)
