462. Isabelle27 TAILLEFER of ANGOULEME (Aymer28 TAILLEFER) was born in Bordeaux about 1188. Isabelle died 31 MAY 1246 in Fontevraud, at 57 years of age. Her body was interred in Fontevraud Abbey.

She married twice. She married JOHN "Lackland" PLANTAGENET in Bordeaux, 24 AUG 1200. JOHN was born in Beaumont Palace, Oxford 24 DEC 1167. JOHN(314) was the son of HENRY II PLANTAGENET and Eleanor of AQUITAINE. JOHN died 18 OCT 1216 in Newark Castle, at 48 years of age. His body was interred in Worcester Cathedral. She married Hugh X of Lusignan, Count of La Marche in France, before 22 MAY 1220.
