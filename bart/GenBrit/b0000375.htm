542. Maud28 ST.HILLARY (James29) birth date unknown. Maud died 1173.

She married Roger "The Good Earl" deCLARE, 3rd Earl of HEREFORD. Roger was born before 1116. Roger was the son of Richard Fitzgilbert deCLARE 1st Earl of HEREFORD and Adela (Adeliza) deMESCHIN of CHESTER. Roger died 1173 at 58 years of age. (See Roger "The Good Earl" deCLARE, 3rd Earl of HEREFORD for the children resulting from this marriage.)
