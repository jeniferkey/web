32. Mary Victoria Augusta5 of TECK (Francis6 Duke of TECK) was born in Kensington Pal., London, England 28 MAY 1867. Mary died 24 MAR 1953 in Marlborough Hse., London, England, at 85 years of age. Her body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor.

She married GEORGE V Albert Guelph WINDSOR, Duke of YORK 6 JUL 1893. GEORGE was born in Marlborough Hse., London, England 3 JUN 1865. GEORGE(20) was the son of EDWARD VII Albert Guelph SAXE-COBURG and Alexandra of Denmark. GEORGE died 20 JAN 1936 in Sandringham House, Norfolk, England, at 70 years of age. His body was interred in St. George's Chapel, Windsor. (See GEORGE V Albert Guelph WINDSOR, Duke of YORK for the children resulting from this marriage.)
