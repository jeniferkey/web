726. Aelfled31 of BERNICIA (ALDRED32) birth date unknown.

She married Sigurd (Siward) MacMALDRED, Earl of HUNTINGDON. Sigurd(519) was the son of Maldred MacCRINAN Lord of CARLISLE & ALLERDALE and (Edith) EALDGYTH. Sigurd died 1055. (See Sigurd (Siward) MacMALDRED, Earl of HUNTINGDON for the children resulting from this marriage.)
