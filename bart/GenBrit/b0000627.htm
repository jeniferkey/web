728. Adelaide31 deGAND, Countess d'AUMALE (Robert II "Le Diable"32 6th Duke of Normandy, Richard II "Le Bon"33 4th Duke of Normandy, Richard I "The Fearless"34 3rd Duke of Normandy, William35 ) was born 1030. Adelaide died before 1090.

She married three times. She married Lambert of LENS. Lambert(522) was the son of Eustace I of BOULOGNE and Maud of LOUVAIN. Lambert died 1054.(523) She married Eudes (Odo) of CHAMPAGNE. She married Enguerrand II Count of PONTHIEU. Enguerrand was the son of Enguerrand I Count of PONTHIEU and Adele HOLLAND. Enguerrand died 1053.
