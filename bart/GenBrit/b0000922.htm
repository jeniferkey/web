1112. LOUIS IV "Outre-Mer"35 (CHARLES III "The Simple"36, LOUIS II "The Stammerer"37) was born about 919. LOUIS died 10 SEP 954 at 35 years of age.

He married Gerberga of SAXONY. Gerberga is the daughter of HENRY I "The Fowler" AUCEPS Duke of SAXONY and Mechtilde of RINGELHEIM.
