355. Isabel (Matilda)24 of MAR ((David) Donald25 macWILLIAM, Earl of MAR, William26 macDUNCAN, Earl of MAR, Duncan27 macMORGUND, Earl of MAR, Morgund28 macGYLOCHER, Earl of MAR, GILLOCHER29 )(243) birth date unknown. Isabel died 1297.

She married ROBERT I BRUCE, King of Scots about 1295. ROBERT was born in Writtle, Essex, England 27 MAR 1274 (or 11 JUL 1274). ROBERT(244) was the son of Robert BRUCE of CLEVELAND & ANNANDALE and Marjorie Martha Margaret DOWELL. ROBERT died 7 JUN 1329 in Cardross near Dumbarton, Scotland. His body was interred in Dunfermline, Scotland. (See ROBERT I BRUCE, King of Scots for the children resulting from this marriage.) of Mar
