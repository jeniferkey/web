1114. Gerberga35 of SAXONY (HENRY I "The Fowler"35 AUCEPS, Duke of SAXONY, OTHO (Otto) "The Illustrious"36 Duke of SAXONY, Ludolph37, Bruno38) birth date unknown.

She married twice. She married Giselbert Duke of LORRAINE. Giselbert was the son of Regnier I Duke of LORRAINE Count of HAINAULT and Hersent of France. Giselbert died 939. She married LOUIS IV "Outre-Mer". LOUIS was born about 919. LOUIS was the son of CHARLES III "The Simple" and Eadgifu (Edgiva). LOUIS died 10 SEP 954 at 35 years of age.

Giselbert Duke of LORRAINE and Gerberga of SAXONY had the following children:

+ 1051 i. Gerberga34 of LORRAINE.

+ 1115 ii. Alberade of LORRAINE.
