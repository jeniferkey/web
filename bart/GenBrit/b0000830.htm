1295. Baldwin II37 Count of Flanders, ARTOIS & BOULOGNE (Baldwin I "Bras-de-Fer"38 of Flanders) was born about 865. Baldwin died 2 JAN 918(19). His body was interred in Abbey of Stes. Peter & Paul.

He married Elfthryth 884. Elfthryth was the daughter of ALFRED "The Great" and Ealhswith. Elfthryth died 7 JUN 929.
