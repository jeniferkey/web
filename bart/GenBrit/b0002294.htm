Matilda29 (HENRY I "The Lion of Justice"30, WILLIAM I "The Conqueror"31 7th Duke of Normandy, Robert II "Le Diable"32 6th Duke of Normandy, Richard II "Le Bon"33 4th Duke of Normandy, Richard I "The Fearless"34 3rd Duke of Normandy, William35 )(402) was born in Winchester about 1103/1104. Matilda died 10 SEP 1167 in Rouen, at 63 years of age.

She married twice. She married HENRY V in Mainz, 7 JAN 1114. (Additional notes for HENRY V(403)) HENRY died 23 MAY 1125 in Utrecht. She married Geoffrey V PLANTAGENET of ANJOU in Le Mans, 22 MAY 1127. Geoffrey was born 23 AUG 1113. Geoffrey(404) was the son of FULK V "The Young" of ANJOU and Erembourg. Geoffrey died 7 SEP 1151 in Chateau-du-Loire, at 38 years of age. Declared heiress-presumptive to Henry I 1126; disputed the throne with King Stephen.
