1067. Rosele (Susanna)34 (BERENGARIUS II35, ADALBERT36) birth date unknown. Rosele died 26 JAN 1003.

She married (Arnold) Arnulf II "The Young" Count of Flanders 968. (Arnold) was born 961(62). (Arnold)(788) was the son of (Baudoin) Baldwin III Count of Flanders and Matilda (Mahaud) deBOURGOGNE (BURGUNDY). (Arnold) died 23 MAR 987. His body was interred 30 MAR 987 in Chapel of St, Laurent, Abbey of St.Pierre. (See (Arnold) Arnulf II "The Young" Count of Flanders for the children resulting from this marriage.)
