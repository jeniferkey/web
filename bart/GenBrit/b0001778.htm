304. Sir_Thomas23 de la HAYE, 7th Baron of ERROLL birth date unknown. Sir_Thomas died JUL 1406. His body was interred in Coupar Angus Abbey.

He married Elizabeth STEWART, Princess of Scotland before 7 NOV 1372. Elizabeth is the daughter of ROBERT II STEWART King of Scots and Elizabeth MURE (MUIR).

Sir_Thomas de la HAYE, 7th Baron of ERROLL and Elizabeth STEWART, Princess of Scotland had the following children:

305 i. Sir_Gilbert22 de la HAY. He married twice. He married an unknown person. He married Elizabeth REID about 1416.

306 ii. William de la HAY, Lord HAY. William died about 1437. He married Margaret GRAY of BROXMOUTH.
