793. Amice31 deMONTFORT (Ralph32 deWAER deMONTFORT, Earl NORFOLK, SUFFOLK, Ralph deGAEL33 deMONTFORT, Earl of NORFOLK & SUFFOLK, Ralph34 Earl of NORFOLK & SUFFOLK) birth date unknown.

She married Robert "Robin the Hunchback" deBEAUMONT, 2nd Earl of LEICESTER after 1120. Robert was born 1104. Robert(570) was the son of Robert deBEAUMONT 1st Earl of LEICESTER and Isabel deVERMANDOIS. Robert died 5 APR 1166 at 61 years of age. (See Robert "Robin the Hunchback" deBEAUMONT, 2nd Earl of LEICESTER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
