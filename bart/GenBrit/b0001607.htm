93. Frederick Louis9 HANOVER, Prince of Wales (GEORGE II Augustus Guelph10 HANOVER, GEORGE I Louis Guelph11, Ernest Augustus12 Duke of BRUNSWICK-LUNEBURG)(37) was born in Hanover, Germany 20 JAN 1707. Frederick died 20 MAR 1751 in Leicester House, London, at 44 years of age.

He married Augusta of SAXE-GOTHA in Chapel Royal, St. James' palace, 27 APR 1736. Augusta was born in Gotha 30 NOV 1719. Augusta was the daughter of Frederick II of SAXE-GOTHA and Magdelena Augusta. Augusta died 8 FEB 1772 in Carlton House, London, at 52 years of age. referred to as "Poor Fred"

Frederick Louis HANOVER, Prince of Wales and Augusta of SAXE-GOTHA had the following children:

94 i. Augusta8 HANOVER was born in St. James' Palace 31 JUL 1737. Augusta died 23 MAR 1813 in St. James' Palace, at 75 years of age. She married Charles II Duke of BRUNSWICK-WOLFENBUTTEL in St. James' Palace, 16 JAN 1764.

+ 72 ii. GEORGE III HANOVER was born 4 JUN 1738.

96 iv. Elizabeth Caroline HANOVER was born in Norfolk House, London 30 DEC 1740. Elizabeth died 4 SEP 1759 in Kew Palace, at 18 years of age.

97 v. William Henry HANOVER, Duke of GLOUCESTER & Edinbur was born in Leicester House, London 14 NOV 1743. William died 25 AUG 1805 in Gloucester House, London, at 61 years of age. He married Maria WALPOLE in London, 6 SEP 1766. Maria was born in Westminster 3 JUL 1736. Maria was the daughter of Edward WALPOLE and Dorothy CLEMENT. Maria died 22 AUG 1807 in Gloucester Lodge, Brompton, at 71 years of age.

98 vi. Henry Frederick HANOVER, Duke of CUMBERLAND was born in Leicester House, London 27 OCT 1745. Henry died 18 SEP 1790 in London, at 44 years of age. He married Anne LUTTRELL in London, 2 OCT 1771. Anne was born in London 24 JAN 1743. Anne was the daughter of Simon LUTTRELL 1st Earl CARHAMPTON and Judith Maria LAWES. Anne died 28 DEC 1808 in Trieste, at 65 years of age.

99 vii. Louisa Anne HANOVER was born in Leicester House, London 8 MAR 1749. Louisa died 13 MAY 1768 in Carlton House, at 19 years of age.

100 viii. Frederick William HANOVER was born in Leicester House 13 MAY 1750. Frederick died 28 DEC 1765 in Leicester House, at 15 years of age.

101 ix. Caroline Matilda HANOVER was born in Leicester House 11 JUL 1751. Caroline died 10 MAY 1775 in Zell, Germany, at 23 years of age. She married CHRISTIAN VII of Denmark in Christiansborg, 8 NOV 1766. (Additional notes for CHRISTIAN VII of Denmark(38))
