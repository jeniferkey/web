388. Marguerite d'ANJOU diNAPOLI25 CAPET of SICILY (Charles II "The Lame" Duc of PROVENCE26 CAPET, King of SICILY, Charles I d'ANJOU Count of PROVENCE27 CAPET, King of, Louis VIII28 CAPET, King of FRANCE) birth date unknown.

She married Charles CAPET, Count of VALOIS. Charles was the son of PHILIP III CAPET King of France and Isabella of ARAGON. Charles died 1325. (See Charles CAPET, Count of VALOIS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
