102. Augusta9 of SAXE-GOTHA (Frederick II10) was born in Gotha 30 NOV 1719. Augusta died 8 FEB 1772 in Carlton House, London, at 52 years of age.

She married Frederick Louis HANOVER, Prince of Wales in Chapel Royal, St. James' palace, 27 APR 1736. Frederick was born in Hanover, Germany 20 JAN 1707. Frederick(39) was the son of GEORGE II Augustus Guelph HANOVER and Caroline of ANSPACH. Frederick died 20 MAR 1751 in Leicester House, London, at 44 years of age. (See Frederick Louis HANOVER, Prince of Wales for the children resulting from this marriage.)
