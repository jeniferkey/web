504. David28 MacHENRY, Earl of HUNTINGDON & CHESTER (Prince Henry28 MacDAVID, Earl of HUNTINGDON, DAVID I29 , MALCOLM III "Canmore"30, DUNCAN I31 MacCRINAN, CRINAN "The Thane"32 ) was born 1144. David died 17 JUN 1219 in Yardley, England, at 74 years of age.

He married Maud (Matilda) deGUERNAN of CHESTER 26 AUG 1190. Maud was born 1171. Maud(370) was the daughter of Hugh deGUERNAN 3rd Earl of CHESTER and Bertrade deMONTFORT. Maud died 1233 at 62 years of age.

SOURCE NOTES: _A History of Wales_, John Davies, p.141. Heir of Rannulf, Earl of Chester, the most powerful of the Marcher lords in the time of Llywelyn. On John's death without issue, Chester passed into the hands of the English Crown, in the person of Edward I, facilitating that king's eventual domination of Wales.
