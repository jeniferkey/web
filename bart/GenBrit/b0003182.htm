528. Gertrude28 LUIPOLDING de MERAN birth date unknown.

She married ANDREAS II of Jerusalem ARPAD, King of Hungary. ANDREAS was the son of BELA III ARPAD King of Hungary. ANDREAS died 1235. (See ANDREAS II of Jerusalem ARPAD, King of Hungary for the children resulting from this marriage.)
