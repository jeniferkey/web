649. Judith30 of LENS (Lambert31, Eustace I32 of BOULOGNE) was born 3) 1054. Judith died after 1086.

She married Waltheof II Earl of HUNTINGDON & NORTHUMBERLAND 1070. Waltheof was born about 1045. Waltheof(445) was the son of Sigurd (Siward) MacMALDRED Earl of HUNTINGDON and Aelfled of BERNICIA. Waltheof died 31 MAY 1076 in Winchester, England, at 30 years of age.(446) (See Waltheof II Earl of HUNTINGDON & NORTHUMBERLAND for the children resulting from this marriage.)
