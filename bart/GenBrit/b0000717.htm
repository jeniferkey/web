783. Beatrix31 deGHENT (Henry32) birth date unknown.

She married Alberic "Sanglie" deVERE, Count AUBREY. Alberic(562) was the son of Alphonso deVERE Count de GHESNES and unknown. Alberic died before 1139. (See Alberic "Sanglie" deVERE, Count AUBREY for the children resulting from this marriage.)
