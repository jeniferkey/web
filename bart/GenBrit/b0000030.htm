359. Jean25 of the Isles (James26 of BUTE)(246) birth date unknown.

She married Alexander STEWART, 4th High Steward of Scotland. Alexander was the son of Walter STEWART 3rd High Steward of Scotland and Beatrix of ANGUS. Alexander died about 1283. (See Alexander STEWART, 4th High Steward of Scotland for the children resulting from this marriage.) Heiress of Bute, Heiress of Arran
