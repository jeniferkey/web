1045. Gerlote (Gerloc, Adele)34 of NORMANDY (Rolf (Rollo) (Robert I) "The Ganger"35 , Rognwald36 Jarl of MAERC, Eyestein37 Jarl of MEARC, Ivar38 Earl of the Uplands, Halfdan "The Old"39 , SVEIDE "The Viking"40) birth date unknown. Gerlote died about 14 OCT 962.
