1206. Ludgarde (Hedwige)36 of Germany (ARNULF37 , CARLOMAN38, LOUIS "The German"39) birth date unknown.

She married OTHO (Otto) "The Illustrious" Duke of SAXONY. OTHO was the son of Ludolph Duke of SAXONY and Hedwige (Oda) of FRIEUL. OTHO died 12 NOV 912. (See OTHO (Otto) "The Illustrious" Duke of SAXONY for the children resulting from this marriage.)
