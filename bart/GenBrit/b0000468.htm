1051. Gerberga34 of LORRAINE (Gerberga35 of SAXONY) birth date unknown.

She married Albert I deVERMANDOIS. Albert was born about 920. Albert was the son of Herbert II deVERMANDOIS Count of TROYES and Liegarde (Hildebrant) CAPET. Albert died about 988. (See Albert I deVERMANDOIS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
