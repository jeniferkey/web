40. Julia6 von HAUKE, Princess of BATTENBURG was born 1825. Julia died 1885 at 60 years of age.

She married Alexander. Alexander was born 1823. Alexander was the son of Ludwig II Grand Duke of HESSE and Wilhelmina. Alexander died 1888 at 65 years of age. (See Alexander for the children resulting from this marriage.)
