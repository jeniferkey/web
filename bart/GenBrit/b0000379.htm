487. Gilbert27 deCLARE, Earl of HEREFORD & GLOUCESTER (Richard28 deCLARE, Earl of HEREFORD & CLOUCESTER)(324) was born about 1180. Gilbert died 25 OCT 1230 in Penros, Brittany, at 50 years of age. His body was interred 10 NOV 1230 in Tewkewbury, England.

He married Isabel MARSHALL of PEMBROKE 9 OCT 1217. Isabel was the daughter of William MARSHALL 3rd Earl of PEMBROKE and Isabel deCLARE of PEMBROKE. Isabel died 17 JAN 1239(40) in Berkhamstead, England. 7th Earl of Clare, etc.; surety for Magna Carta
