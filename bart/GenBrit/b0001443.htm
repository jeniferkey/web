ROBERT died 4 APR 1406 in Dundonald, Scotland, at 68 years of age.(170) RESEARCH NOTES: Said to have died of starvation in his grief at the capture by the English of his son, James (later James I of Scotland).

He married twice. He married an unknown person. He married Annabella DRUMMOND about 1367. Annabella died about 1401.
