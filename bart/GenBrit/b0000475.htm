921. Gerberga33 of LORRAINE (Charles34 , LOUIS IV "Outre-Mer"35, CHARLES III "The Simple"36, LOUIS II "The Stammerer"37) was born about 990. Gerberga died 1015 at 25 years of age.

She married Lambert I "The Bearded" of LOUVAIN. Lambert(667) was the son of Regnier III Count of HAINAULT and Adele of DAGSBOURG. Lambert died 1015. (See Lambert I "The Bearded" of LOUVAIN for the children resulting from this marriage.)
