802. Ealdgyth32 (Sigeferth33) birth date unknown.

She married EDMUND II "Ironside" SUMMER 1015. EDMUND was born 989. EDMUND(578) was the son of ETHELRED II "The Unready" and Elflaed. EDMUND died 30 NOV 1016 in Oxford. His body was interred in Glastonbury Abbey. (See EDMUND II "Ironside" for the children resulting from this marriage.)
