623. Hugh29 deGUERNAN, 3rd Earl of CHESTER (Ranulf "de Briquessard"30 deGUERNAN, 2nd Earl of CHESTER)(426) was born 1147. Hugh died 1181 in Leeke Co, Stafford, England, at 34 years of age.

He married Bertrade deMONTFORT 1169. Bertrade was born 1155. Bertrade was the daughter of Simon deMONTFORT Count d'EVREUX and Maud. Bertrade died 1227 at 72 years of age. of Kevilioc

Hugh deGUERNAN, 3rd Earl of CHESTER and Bertrade deMONTFORT had the following children:

624 i. Mabel28 deGUERNAN(427). may have been a Crusader

625 ii. Agnes deGUERNAN(428). Agnes died 2 NOV 1247. Lady of Chartley

626 iii. Ranulf deGUERNAN, 4th Earl of CHESTER.

+ 563 iv. Maud (Matilda) deGUERNAN of CHESTER was born 1171.

627 v. Hawise deGUERNAN(429) was born 1180. Hawise died 3) 1242. Countess of Lincoln
