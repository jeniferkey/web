444. Isabelle26 deCLARE (Gilbert27 deCLARE, Earl of HEREFORD & GLOUCESTER, Richard28 deCLARE, Earl of HEREFORD & CLOUCESTER)(299) was born 1226. Isabelle died after 1284.

She married Robert "The Competitor" BRUCE, 5th Earl of ANNANDAL about 1240. Robert was born 1210?. Robert was the son of Robert BRUCE 4th Lord of ANNANDALE and Isabel "The Scot" of HUNTINGDON. Robert died 1295 in Lochmaben Castle, Dumfrieshire, Scotland. (See Robert "The Competitor" BRUCE, 5th Earl of ANNANDAL for the children resulting from this marriage.) was 13 years old at time of marriage
