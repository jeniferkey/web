632. Fergus29 of GALLOWAY birth date unknown. Fergus died 12 MAY 1166.

He married Elizabeth FITZHENRY. Elizabeth(433) was the daughter of HENRY I "The Lion of Justice" and unknown. Her body was interred in Holyrood Abbey.

Fergus of GALLOWAY and Elizabeth FITZHENRY had the following children:

+ 566 i. Gilbert28 MacFERGUS.

633 ii. Uchtred MacFERGUS of GALLOWAY. Uchtred died 1174. He married Gunnild of DUNBAR. Gunnild is the daughter of Waltheof of ALLERDALE and Sigrid.
