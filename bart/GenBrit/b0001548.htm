144. Lady_Margaret16 DOUGLAS of ANGUS (Archibald17 DOUGLAS, 6th Earl of ANGUS, George18 DOUGLAS, Master of ANGUS, Archibald "Bell-the-Cat"19 DOUGLAS, 5th Earl of ANGUS, George20 DOUGLAS, 4th Earl of ANGUS, William21 DOUGLAS, 2nd Earl of ANGUS, George22 DOUGLAS, 1st Earl of ANGUS) birth date unknown. Lady_Margaret died 9 MAR 1577/1578.

She married Matthew STEWART, 13th Earl of LENNOX 29 JUN 1544. Matthew(76) was the son of John STEWART 12th Earl of LENNOX and Elizabeth STEWART of ATHOLL. Matthew died 1571. (See Matthew STEWART, 13th Earl of LENNOX for the children resulting from this marriage.)
