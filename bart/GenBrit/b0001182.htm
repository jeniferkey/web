1385. Guelph (Welf) I39 of Bavaria (Isenbart40 of ALTORF, Warinus41, Everhard42 of ALSATIA, Adalbertus43)(1072) was born 787. Guelph died CA 818(820).

He married Edith (Hedwig) of SAXONY. Duke of Bavaria, Count of Altorf (Andech)

Guelph (Welf) I of Bavaria and Edith (Hedwig) of SAXONY had the following children:

+ 1328 i. Judith38 of Bavaria.

+ 1386 ii. Emma of Spain.

+ 1331 iii. Conrad I of ALTORF & BURGUNDY was born 800.

1387 iv. Ethico of ALTORF & RAVENSBURG. Ethico died 870. He married Judith. Judith is the daughter of ETHELWULF ("Noble Wolf") and Osburgh (Osburga).
