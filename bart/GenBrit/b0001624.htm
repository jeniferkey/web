189. Elizabeth19 BOYD of BOYD birth date unknown.

She married Archibald "Bell-the-Cat" DOUGLAS, 5th Earl of ANGUS 4 MAR 1467/1468. Archibald was the son of George DOUGLAS 4th Earl of ANGUS and Isabel SIBBALD of BALGONY. Archibald died 29 NOV 1513. (See Archibald "Bell-the-Cat" DOUGLAS, 5th Earl of ANGUS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
