945. Ermengarde33 of BARR (Reinald34) birth date unknown.

She married Herbert III deVERMANDOIS. Herbert was born about 955. Herbert was the son of Albert I deVERMANDOIS and Gerberga of LORRAINE. Herbert died about 1000. (See Herbert III deVERMANDOIS for the children resulting from this marriage.)
