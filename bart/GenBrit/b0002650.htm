1099. William34 FITZOSBERN (Osbern35 , Herfast36 deCREPON)(818) birth date unknown. William died FEB 1071 in Cassel.(819) RESEARCH NOTES: killed in battle

He married Adeliza. Adeliza is the daughter of Roger I of TOSNY and Godehildis. Steward to William I "Conqueror"; fought at Hastings, 1066; received large estates in England; made Earl of Hereford shortly after 1066.

William FITZOSBERN and Adeliza had the following children:

1100 i. William33 FITZOSBERN(820). received the Norman Fitz-Osbern lands

1101 ii. Roger of BRETEUIL(821). Roger died after 1089. Earl of Hereford, 1071; received the English Fitz-Osbern lands; deprived of his earldom after the rebellion of 1075.

+ 1018 iii. Emma FITZOSBERN.
