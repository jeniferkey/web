498. Eschyne28 deCLARE deMEULLES birth date unknown.

She married Walter FITZALAN, 1st High Steward of Scotland. Walter(328) was the son of Alan FITZFLAALD Lord OSWESTRY and Ameline deWARENNE. Walter died about 1177. (See Walter FITZALAN, 1st High Steward of Scotland for the children resulting from this marriage.)
