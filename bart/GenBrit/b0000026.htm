414. Beatrix26 of ANGUS (Gilchrist27 Earl of ANGUS)(281) birth date unknown.

She married Walter STEWART, 3rd High Steward of Scotland. Walter was born in Scotland. Walter(282) was the son of Alan FITZWALTER 2nd High Steward of Scotland and Eve SWEYNSDOTTER. Walter died 1) 1246. (See Walter STEWART, 3rd High Steward of Scotland for the children resulting from this marriage.) poss. Alda de Dunbar
