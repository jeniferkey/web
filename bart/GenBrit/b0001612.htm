71. Victoire Mary Louisa8 of SAXE-COBURG-SAALFELD (Francis Frederick Anthony9 Duke of SAXE-COBURG-SAALF) was born in Coburg 17 AUG 1786. Victoire died 18 MAR 1861 in Frogmore House, Windsor, at 74 years of age.

She married twice. She married Emich Charles 2nd Prince of LEININGEN. She married Edward Augustus Guelph HANOVER, Duke of KENT in Kew Palace, 20 MAY 1818.(32) RESEARCH NOTES: Also: 11 JUL 1818

Edward was born in Buckingham House 2 NOV 1767. Edward was the son of GEORGE III HANOVER and Charlotte Sophia of MECKLENBERG-STRELITZ. Edward died 23 JAN 1820 in Sidmouth, Devon, at 52 years of age.
