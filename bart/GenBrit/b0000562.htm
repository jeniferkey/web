LOUIS I "The Pious" "The Fair"38 (CHARLEMAGNE39, PEPIN "The Short"40, Charles41 MARTEL, Pepin II "The Younger"42 of HERISTAL, Ansgise (Ansegis)43 , Arnulf44, Bodegisel II45 of METZ, Bodegisel I46 , Munderic47 of VITRY-EN-PERTHOIS, CLODERIC "The Parricide"48 , SIGEBERT "The Lame"49, CHILDEBERT50 of COLOGNE, CLOVIS "The Riparian"51)(1026) was born 778. LOUIS died 20 JUN 840.

He married twice. He married Ermengarde 769. Ermengarde was the daughter of Ingram of HASBANIA and unknown. Ermengarde died 818. He married Judith of Bavaria 19 APR 819. Judith was the daughter of Guelph (Welf) I of Bavaria and Edith (Hedwig) of SAXONY. Judith died 19 APR 833. King of France, Germany & Italy; Emperor 814-840
