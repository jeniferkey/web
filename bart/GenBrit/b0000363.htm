770. Rohese (Rose)31 GIFFARD (Walter32 deBOLBEC GIFFARD, Osbern33 deBOLBEC) birth date unknown.

She married Richard FITZGILBERT, 1st Earl of CLARE. Richard was born before 1035. Richard(554) was the son of Gilbert (Giselbert) Count of BRIONNE and unknown. Richard died 1090 at 56 years of age. (See Richard FITZGILBERT, 1st Earl of CLARE for the children resulting from this marriage.)
