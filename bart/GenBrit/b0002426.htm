280. Catherine22 SWYNFORD (Payn23 ROET) was born 1350. Catherine died 10 MAY 1403 in Lincoln, at 52 years of age.

She married twice. She married Hugh SWYNFORD. Hugh died before 1396. She married John of Gaunt PLANTAGENET, Duke of LANCASTER in Lincoln, 13 JAN 1396. John was born in Abbey of St. Bavon, Ghent MAR 1340. John(189) was the son of EDWARD III PLANTAGENET and Philippa of HAINAULT. John died 3 FEB 1399 in Leicester Castle, at 58 years of age.
