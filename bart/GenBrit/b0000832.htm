Baldwin died 1 SEP 1067 in Lilles, France, at 55 years of age. His body was interred in Collegiate Church of St. Pierre.

He married Adela of France. Adela was the daughter of ROBERT II "The Pious" CAPET and Constance of TOULOUSE. Adela died 8 JAN 1079. Count of Flanders.
