1344. Eberhard38 Count of BURGUNDY (Henroch (Hunrock)39 of FRIEUL) birth date unknown. Eberhard died about 864.

He married Gisela of France. Gisela was born 820. Gisela was the daughter of LOUIS I "The Pious" "The Fair" and Judith of Bavaria. Gisela died 1 JUL 874.

Eberhard Count of BURGUNDY and Gisela of France had the following children:

+ 1298 i. BERENGER I37 was born 850.

+ 1271 ii. Hedwige (Oda) of FRIEUL.
