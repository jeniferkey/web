490. Isabel27 MARSHALL of PEMBROKE (William28 MARSHALL, 3rd Earl of PEMBROKE, John29 leMARSCHAL, Gilbert30) birth date unknown. Isabel died 17 JAN 1239(40) in Berkhamstead, England.

She married Gilbert deCLARE, Earl of HEREFORD & GLOUCESTER 9 OCT 1217. Gilbert was born about 1180. Gilbert(326) was the son of Richard deCLARE Earl of HEREFORD & CLOUCESTER and Amice (Amica) of GLOUCESTER. Gilbert died 25 OCT 1230 in Penros, Brittany, at 50 years of age. His body was interred 10 NOV 1230 in Tewkewbury, England. (See Gilbert deCLARE, Earl of HEREFORD & GLOUCESTER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
