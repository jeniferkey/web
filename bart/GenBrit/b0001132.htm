1126. HENRY I "The Fowler"35 AUCEPS, Duke of SAXONY (OTHO (Otto) "The Illustrious"36 Duke of SAXONY, Ludolph37, Bruno38)(851) was born 876. HENRY died 2 JUL 936 in Memleben.

He married Mechtilde of RINGELHEIM. Mechtilde was the daughter of Deitrich Count of RINGELHEIM and unknown. Mechtilde died 969. Emperor of Germany 919-936

HENRY I "The Fowler" AUCEPS, Duke of SAXONY and Mechtilde of RINGELHEIM had the following children:

+ 1043 i. Hedwig34 of SAXONY.

+ 1114 ii. Gerberga of SAXONY.

1127 iii. daughter.

1128 iv. OTTO I "The Great"(852) was born 916. OTTO died 973. He married twice. He married Adalhyde of BURGUNDY 951. He married Eadgyth. Eadgyth is the daughter of EDWARD I "The Elder" and Elfleda. Emperor of Germany 936-973
