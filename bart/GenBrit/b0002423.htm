273. John of Gaunt22 PLANTAGENET, Duke of LANCASTER (EDWARD III23 PLANTAGENET, EDWARD II24, EDWARD I "Longshanks"25, HENRY III26, JOHN "Lackland"27, HENRY II28, Geoffrey V29 PLANTAGENET of ANJOU, FULK V "The Young"30 of ANJOU, Fulk IV "Rechin"31, Aubri-Geoffrey32 of the GATINAIS, Geoffrey III33)(180) was born in Abbey of St. Bavon, Ghent MAR 1340. John died 3 FEB 1399 in Leicester Castle, at 58 years of age.

He married three times. He married Blanche of LANCASTER in Reading, 13 MAY 1359. Blanche was born 1341. Blanche(181) was the daughter of Henry PLANTAGENET 1st Duke of LANCASTER and Isabel deBEAUMONT. Blanche died 12 SEP 1369 in Bolingbroke Castle, at 28 years of age. He married Constanza of CASTILE in Roquefort, SEP 1371. Constanza was the daughter of Pedro. Constanza died 24 MAR 1394 in Leicester. He married Catherine SWYNFORD in Lincoln, 13 JAN 1396. Catherine was born 1350. Catherine was the daughter of Payn ROET. Catherine died 10 MAY 1403 in Lincoln, at 52 years of age. Titular King of Castile

His body was interred in Canterbury Cathedral. He married twice. He married Mary deBOHUN in Arundel Castle, 1380. (Additional notes for Mary deBOHUN(184)) Mary died 4 JUL 1394 in Peterborough Castle.(185) He married Joan in Winchester Cathedral, 7 FEB 1403. Joan was born in Pamplona about 1370. Created Duke of Hereford, 1397; King of England 1399-1413. First of the Lancastrian kings of England.
