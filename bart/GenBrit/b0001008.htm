Edward "The Exile"31 Atheling (EDMUND II "Ironside"32 , ETHELRED II "The Unready"33, EDGAR "The Peaceful"34, EDMUND I "The Magnificent"35, EDWARD I "The Elder"36, ALFRED "The Great"37, ETHELWULF ("Noble Wolf")38, EGBERT39, EAHLMUND40, EAFA41, EOPPA42, INGILD43, CENRED44, CEOWALD45, CUTHA46, CUTHWINE47, (Ceolin)48 CAEELIN, CYNRIC49 , CERDIC50)(511) was born after 1015. Edward died 1057 at 41 years of age.

He married twice. He married Agatha. Agatha(512) is the daughter of Bruno of Hungary and unknown. He married Anastasia. Anastasia(513) was the daughter of Jaroslaus I of KIEV and Ingegard of Sweden. Anastasia died after 1064. twin; sent to Hungary (i.e., away from Canute)
