1129. Mechtilde35 of RINGELHEIM (Deitrich36 Count of RINGELHEIM, Walpert37 Count of RINGELHEIM & OLDENBURG, Wigbert38 Duke of SAXONY, WITIKIND I39 , WERNICKE40) birth date unknown. Mechtilde died 969.

She married HENRY I "The Fowler" AUCEPS, Duke of SAXONY. HENRY was born 876. HENRY(853) was the son of OTHO (Otto) "The Illustrious" Duke of SAXONY and Ludgarde (Hedwige) of Germany. HENRY died 2 JUL 936 in Memleben. (See HENRY I "The Fowler" AUCEPS, Duke of SAXONY for the children resulting from this marriage.)
