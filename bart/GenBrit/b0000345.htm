686. Lucia (Lucy)30 TAILLEBOIS (Ivo31) birth date unknown. Lucia died about 1136.

She married Ranulf "de Briquessart" deMESCHIN, 1st Earl of CHESTER about 1098. Ranulf was born before 1066. Ranulf(481) was the son of Ranulf deMESCHIN Vicomte de BAYEUX and Maud (Margaret Mathilde) leGOZ d'AVARANCHES. Ranulf died CA 1128(29). His body was interred in St. Werburg's, Chester, England. (See Ranulf "de Briquessart" deMESCHIN, 1st Earl of CHESTER for the children resulting from this marriage.)
