Edinburgh Castle

Edinburgh Castle, perched atop its extinct volcanic peak. The entrance is flanked by statues of Robert Bruce and William Wallace.



Edinburgh Castle (entrance detail)

A detailed look at the the entrance to the Castle. The Latin inscription -- a motto of Scotland -- reads: "No one safely provokes me."



Scottish National War Memorial (entrance)

The entrance to the Scottish National War Memorial inside the Castle. The inscription above the entrance reads: "To the Glory of God & in Memory of Scots who Fell, 1914-1918."
